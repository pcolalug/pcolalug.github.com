Title: New Optical Drive
Post by: dpeach on November 28, 2005, 05:58:30 pm
From a company called InPhase Technologies (http://www.inphase-technologies.com/) is a new 3-D holographic optical drive.

Potential storage of 1.6 TB per disk. But they will start with a meager 300 GB. Transfer rates around 120 Mbps. This article (http://news.ft.com/cms/s/82e70cee-6036-11da-a3a6-0000779e2340.html) give a brief explanation. At the InPhase web site you can watch a couple of movies. I have not seen them yet as I don't have sound in Quicktime movies currently working.
