Birds will sing, dogs will bark, noobs will whine.

Honestly, I don't often use grenades in AA, but when I do, 8/10 times I throw flashbangs.  Flashbangs, I would argue, are more deadly than frags in AA.  Why?  Simple, you don't really have to cook them (though you can, for a VERY breif ammount of time).  Since you don't have to cook them, they take less time to use, and therefore, you spend more time with your gun up, ready to kill.

Example, I spend alot of time as a sniper in AA.  Early on, that ment, I spent alot of time getting killed by rushers.  Not anymore.  Now, I'll actually run up, lob a flashbang so that it airbursts near the rusher, then run up, and no-scope the guy while he's helpless and blind  . On other occasions, when I'm unlucky enough to get flashbanged, I imediately pull out and throw both of my flashes.  6/10 the guy that flashed me is caught exposed, running in for the kill, and gets blinded.  Then it's my turn to pop him from behind cover 

About the only time I use frags, is either when the enemy is camping out somewhere, or I have a bud covering me.   Since you have to cook a nade for 5 clicks, it's beter to use against timid, stationary targets.

As for servers, I recommend you start out on the non-sf maps, and hone your skills on mout, insurgent camp, bridge, weapons cache and the like.
