Title: Sharp Unveils Next-Gen Zaurus
Post by: dpeach on November 26, 2003, 10:08:40 am
It is hitting the shelves in Japan around December 5 (the first model). There will be three models. They should hit the US sometime in the first quarter of 2004.

6000N (no wi-fi or bluetooth)
6000L (wi-fi, no bluetooth)
6000W (wi-fi and bluetooth)

Now are you ready for the prices?
6000N $845
6000L $920
6000W $1010

The size is the thing that is knocking many people out (as well as the price). They are: 3.2" by 6.2" by .8". The original 5500 is considered large at 5.5" tall. The new one is almost 3/4" longer, more than 1/4" wider than the current Z, but only a scotch thicker. Weight depends on the model and ranges from 8.9 ounces to 9.3 ounces.

It is geared more towards an industrial environment. They are "ruggedized" to withstand normal droppage and wear and tear.

That is the quick and dirty on them. I know quite a few more facts, but don't want to unnecessarily bore you with them all.
