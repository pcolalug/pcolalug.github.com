Regarding the old machine with only a floppy drive.

I was in a similar situation once.  I turned to debian's netinstall floppies.  Two floppies and a decent internet connection will get you a complete linux install in an hour or two.

Try these:
http://http.us.debian.org/debian/dists/sarge/main/installer-m68k/current//images/floppy/

As for your internet connection, I think you need yourself a real router (i recommend linksys).  Then you can set up your network like this:
cable_modem-->router-->your_computers.  Cox should assign an ip address to your router, and then your router handles your own local network.
