Title: Flashing the FON
Post by: bhoff on November 25, 2007, 09:20:14 pm
Not long ago Fon was giving away routers for free in order to create a free wifi zone around the world. Well I signed up and got one and 1 week later was shutdown by Cox. So what to do with it? I decided to reflash it for private use and get a lot more out of it.
You can also purchase these amazing routers from Fon pretty cheap and the best part is they use the Atheros chip set which the drivers are completely open now. I now have 3 virtual AP's running from one AP giving various level of access depending on who it is and there is ton's more you can do with it. I used OpenWRT for mine but feel free to use this as a guide and use whatever you want ..

The how-to can be found here. http://www.open-sourceenterprises.com/flashthefon.html (http://www.open-sourceenterprises.com/flashthefon.html)
