Title: Blog Software
Post by: dpeach on January 25, 2005, 04:06:33 pm
Have you looked at PHPWebSite (http://phpwebsite.appstate.edu/)? Not really blogging software per se, but has quite a bit of what you are looking for. I have not done too much with it, but have it installed with the thought of converting my whole site over to it.

Advantages that I can see for your situation is that anyone visiting the site can suggest content. But it would be up to an admin to allow the content to be shown. Dealing with teens (even church ones) can turn up content on your church website that you may not want. Having an admin approve each item before it goes up would be a smart move. You are not talking about hundreds of posts a day (I don't think). So taking 10 minutes each day to approve or deny content should not be too time consuming.

Configuring the software and the site to my liking has taken quite a bit of work. But adding content and approving new content is a breeze.

I use it from Firefox on Linux without any issues.
