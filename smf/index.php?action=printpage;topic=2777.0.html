Title: Forum Upgrade
Post by: rugmonster on January 28, 2006, 04:12:55 pm
*NOTE TIME CHANGE*

An update to SMF, version 1.0.6, was released today.  The update addresses a cross-site scripting vulnerability as well as several minor bugs.  I plan to take the forums down for a short bit in the morning after 12am CST to complete the upgrade.

I also wanted to let everyone know about SMF version 1.1 that is currently in final release candidate state.  It currently boasts an improved search engine as well as more flexible search options (searching within a topic, searching through your private messages, etc).  Also, the devs have made use of AJAX for topic editing, so you will be able to preview your post without reloading the entire page.  There are tons of other enhancements I'm sure everyone will find useful.  Once it is officially released, the steering committee will test it and then install it once it meets our seal of approval.  More to follow...
