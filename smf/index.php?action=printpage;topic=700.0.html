Title: Gentoo games
Post by: rburt3 on May 16, 2003, 02:48:55 pm
Seems the folks over at Gentoo (http://www.gentoo.org) have started their own game company. To kick it off, they have a LiveCD with the brand-spanking-new, never-before-seen-on-Linux, full version of America's Army on it.

For those that don't know, a Gentoo LiveCD is similar to Knoppix, in that it is a full distro on a disk. The one mentioned above will let you play AA like your computer was a console system.
