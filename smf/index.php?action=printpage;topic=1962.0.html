Title: GPL Question
Post by: rburt3 on October 26, 2004, 10:23:18 am
Even if the project seems dead, it is courtesy to try and get in touch with the devs and submit a patch. If you can't get any response, you may want to take over the project yourself (with their blessing, of course), or try and find another user/dev to do so. By distributing the project on your own, you give the impression of forking the project, and that could be frowned upon without doing these other things. It won't be illegal, mind you, just in bad taste.
