Title: January 2009 books for review
Post by: dpeach on January 16, 2009, 12:29:15 pm
If you want to order a book from O'Reilly (http://oreilly.com/) to review (which means you get the book for free), please send me a PM with the book title that you want with the ISBN. I need these by the January 29. I will put in the order on the 30th. This way we will have the books at the next meeting.

If you are unfamiliar with the program here are the basics (http://www.pcolalug.org/smf/index.php?topic=624.0).

You can request 1 book at a time and you must complete the review in a timely manner. If you cannot, or choose not to, then please let us know so we can get the book into the hands of someone who can. Our relationship with O'Reilly depends on us giving them reviews for their books.

Do not contact O'Reilly to request books. Your request has to come through the LUG for them to send the books out for free.
