Finally got a chance to goto Mama's Best downtown. Neat clean place. Service was fast. Food was excellent! Has pancit bihon (sooo good!), crispy fried lumpias, fried chicken wings, and rice.. had leche flan for dessert.. My companion had chicken teriyaki w. steamed vegetables, and steamed white rice.. It was all extremely fresh!

Definitely going to go there again. I've also tried Loompia House & Manila Garden a while back.. Was not very happy w. Loompia House, not much very appetizing to me from their buffet.. Manila Garden was definitely better than Loompia House. I enjoyed Mama's Best overall.

Have fun!
