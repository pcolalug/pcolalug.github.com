IT support for NAVSCOLEOD needed at Eglin

Principle Duties and Essential Job Functions:

Participates in highly complex integration projects. Installs, generates, maintains, and debugs system software programs with COTS/GOTS applications and support equipment, develops instructions for integration of operating system software, and modifies/maintains integration packages via releases and utilities from manufacturers. Responsible for applications dealing with the overall operating system, such as file maintenance routines, networks, system software, or software packages. Administers complex operating systems software and complex database management software.

 

Basic Qualifications:

Prefer Bachelor’s with two (2) years related experience or six (6) years in lieu with H.S. Diploma.

 

Experience with desktop computers and software for word processing and database management.  Able to communicate effectively with individuals, using a high degree of tact and diplomacy.

 

Performs systems administration including participating in the software and hardware installations. Administers and maintains assigned systems.  Reviews changes to schedule of operations for successful day-to-day operations.  Optimizes the functionality and performance of hardware and software systems and ensures availability, integrity, efficiency, and reliability of servers.  Identifies system problems that are of an unusual or complex nature and recommend developments for corrective action.  Performs installation and configuration of applications software and the establishment of user environments.  Analyzes and troubleshoots unusual or complex hardware and software malfunctions to resolve physical and logical processing problems and recommends acquisition of equipment, software and/or hardware which will resolve operational problems.  Continually monitors system operation to identify and anticipate potential problems, which could lead to loss or serious interruption of service.  Analyzes and diagnoses system failures to isolate source of problems.  Monitors and coordinates the troubleshooting, correction, and testing of complex or unusual problems involving interface and interoperability of system components such as hardware, system software, and application programs.  Performs installation of system patches and enhancements/updates and ensures system integrity is not compromised.

 

Skills: NMCI, NET (or like COTS), PC and component specifications and configurations, Inventory, and/or MS Office.

 

Clearance: Must have an active SECRET Clearance.
