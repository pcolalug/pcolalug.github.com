Welcome

Hi there,

We've launched a new program we'd like you to share with your group members. Please pass along this news:

Join the O'Reilly Blogger Review Program. We'll send you ebooks and videos in exchange for your honest reviews.

You'll get early access to great books and videos about leading-edge software, technology and social media trends--and have your expert opinions heard as a reviewer. Find all the details here.

PyCon, the Python community conference, is doubling down on technical talks this year from across the spectrum. The official deadline for getting your proposal in has passed, but they've agreed to keep the system in "stealth open" until Nov 3 so the people receiving this newsletter can have a chance to submit a proposal.
Sign up and go to http://us.pycon.org/2011/speaker/ to submit a proposal for either a talk or a tutorial for PyCon 2011.

Google announces Google Code-in, their contest to introduce pre-university students to the many kinds of contributions that make open source software development possible starting Nov 22. They're inviting students worldwide to produce a variety of open source code, documentation, training materials and user experience research. Read the blog post for more info.
