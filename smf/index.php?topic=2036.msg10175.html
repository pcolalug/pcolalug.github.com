This could be a cron job running. cron, is Linux's scheduler that allows it to do housekeeping and other items at specified times. There are a couple of things you can check to see if this is what's happening.

1. Open a terminal window and su so you are root. Type "crontab -l" this will list cron jobs assigned to root. You may or may not see anything here.

2. Go into /etc/cron.daily, /etc/cron.hourly,  and the other cron. directories to see what's scheduled to run and how often.

3. Finally, you can view /var/log/messages to see what's being logged.
