Have had my Vonage service up since April 04. Some issues w. conference calling early on (Belmont, CA-Vonage : Pensacola, FL-Vonage : Clients in NYC etc-POTS) but since then, it's been smooth sailing!
Anywhere within the US & Canada, one flat rate.  I also like the vmail to your email address (it's a .wav file). Neat feature.

Cox modem port to WAN port on Motorola VoIP adapter, VoIP adpater pc port to pc. RJ-11 phone line to line 1 port on VoIP adapter. Done. VoIP adapter picked up the TCPIP settings, and I assume has some form of DHCP/NAT. No software to load on the PC. Did a test of a FTP of a large file, while chatting on the phone, no degradation of quality, so there must be some sort of QoS running to prioritize the traffic.

Was thinking of taking it on the road, to avoid the insane calling fees w. the hotels, but my T-Mobile V300 is just more convenient.
