Title: Flash on Linux problem
Post by: kilar on April 12, 2005, 02:45:12 am
Which distro are you using? I have noticed that Fedora's Flash plug-in (or any of the RPM plug-ins) tend to have audio problems. I have not noticed this problems with the Debian plugins (via apt-get), Slackware, or Gentoo.

Other possibilities are that you have a ton of services in the background hogging up resources, you are running an older version of ALSA (or you are using OSS), or you need to "renice" the system and force ALSA to have a higher priority in your services.
