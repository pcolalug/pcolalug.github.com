I loaded it on two computers and had totally different issues. In the end it works great so far. The first computer with a lower end Soyo 600 dragon board and a Xp2200 gave me fits. I had to vi the XF86Config to get the xserver to start. I had to vi lilo to set the apic to "on" to shutdown, I had to modprobe the usb to get the usb to work. I also had sound and network problems. But on my dual athlon on a GA-7dpxdw-p board, everything configured right on the install. I have had a flaky sound server error about arts overloading the cpu, but the sound works so not sure what thats about. I installed Nvidia drivers, quake3 and point release and it all worked fine.  The layout is much nicer than Mandrake 10.0 . also they fixed the stupid usb 2.0 bug!
