Quote from: Rugmonster
Why should an open source project cease to exist should the founder or project lead leave?

Nobody said it was going to cease to exist.  However, Slackware *as we know it* is /certainly/ in question.

Quote from: Rugmonster
I think there are enough people surrounding these projects to step up and who is to say one of us couldn't be one of those people.

There are certainly enough people who know Slackware Linux well, yes.  But since Slackware Linux is essentially a one-man-show, if he disappears, so does Slackware.  Of course it will continue, under some other name/method.

Quote from: Rugmonster
Rather than be chicken little, let's find out what we can do to keep the goodness going.

Who's being chicken little?  Some of us have already inquired and done some research.  However, there is not really anything that we can do, at this point, as per the wishes of Pat.

Personally, I'm confident he will eventually be OK again.  He's just too important of a guy for things to work out any other way...
