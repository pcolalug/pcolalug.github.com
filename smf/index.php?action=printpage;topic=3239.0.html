Title: Gimp 2.4 released
Post by: dpeach on October 25, 2007, 03:49:42 pm
Gimp 2.4 was released yesterday (http://www.gimp.org/). This is a major update release. We were at 2.2.xx. I have just installed the new version from the packages at Slacky.eu (http://www.slacky.eu/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=4019&Itemid=56). If you have not checked out their site, they have a pretty good selection of Slackware packages.

Of course, Debian users just have to type 'apt-get install gimp' and if your repositories are updated then you will get it easily.

There are new icons for the tools. There are also some new tools in the tools palate. I am sure there are many behind the scenes changes. I have not used it to do any work yet, but for the basic features I use, I am sure it is not much different.

Gimp has also updated their website (http://www.gimp.org/) along with the change.
