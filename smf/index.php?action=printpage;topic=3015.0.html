Title: tonicpoint (linux ppt viewer)
Post by: Brent on September 28, 2006, 11:08:49 pm
Hi all

You may remember, my current distro of choice at the moment is xubuntu.  For my office needs, I simply need something to open word, and power point presentations.  Abiword provides an excelent .doc compatibility, but to view ppt's I've always needed OO's impress.  We all know how bloated and slow OO can be... 

Well, today I was doing a bit of googling, and I came across tonic point.  It's a power point viewer written in java, so it works on windows, linux and mac.  It weighs in at a measly 880k, and handles most of the ppt's i've thrown at it fairly well (though it does have some of the same rendering issues as impress).  I'm very impressed, so impressed, this little jewel is going on my parents home computer (with 64mb of ram). 

http://tonicsystems.com/products/viewer/
