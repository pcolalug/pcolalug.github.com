Firefox really is better
« on: July 13, 2004, 09:35:23 am »
At least five of my co-workers think so. Yesterday, two of the guys asked if there was anything better than IE, complaining it was slow to render pages and the pop-ups...god the pop-ups. Since I use Firefox on every system I touch, I showed them the wonders of tabbed browsing, built in pop-up blocking, adblock and bugmenot.

After showing them where to download it, the basics of how tabbed browsing works and how to get rid of the spyware and adware currently on their systems, we went to our respective homes. This morning, first thing as I walked in the door I was getting raves from five guys about how fast and great Firefox is. They couldn't believe that there was something so great out there for free. They still don't believe me that it's made by volunteers. Now, there is a wave of Firefox excitement going over my office and more people are talking about installing it at home. A small step in my educating others in the joy of OSS, but a step in the right direction.
