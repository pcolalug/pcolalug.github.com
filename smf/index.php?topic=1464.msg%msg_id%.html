I am here to let you all know of our pending web-server move.

to pass some of the responsibility to anyone else. So Jesse has offered to host the Web-server for us.
From this local it may be possible to delegate some of the load to a couple of people vs. one person.
And give a little more control over the web-server. Presently the Domain has been paid up for the nest 5 years by us
in the Steering committee. The only other cost is to have an I.P. address for our site thru COX $60.00 a year. to this end I have sent a request to meet with COX's public relations person to see if they would be willing to sponsor us on this. The second cost is the Web-server to this ends I am donating to the Group a Sun Cobalt RAQ3 server serial no. 3C03AM0265170 It is an AMD350 processor, 128 meg of ram and 16 gig hd rack mount. I am delivering it to Jesse tomorrow in hopes that he can start prepping it for a April 1st move.

I just thought I would make this public and let you know the strides we the steering committee are making to plan for the groups future.
