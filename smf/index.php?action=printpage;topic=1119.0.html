Title: Review: ASP in a Nutshell
Post by: trigger on November 01, 2003, 12:50:44 pm
This book is exactly what it states it is "A Desktop Quick Reference." Within the third day of getting this book. It came to my rescue. It comes in handy with syntax referencing. It continues to come in handy on that 5th hour stretch where syntax is not something you want to be dealing with. The only down side of this book is that it did not come with a cd. I would have paid double to have all those scripts on a searchable CD. Overall, it is a good book. I give it a 4 out of 5 for usefullness.
