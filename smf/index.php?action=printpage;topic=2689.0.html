Title: Re: KVM Switch
Post by: phed on November 04, 2005, 05:29:15 pm
I bought one made by Manhatten at Southeastern Computers on 29.  I use it with one machine running Windows 2000 Pro and the other running SuSe 9.3.  The switch works flawlessly and my Logitech optical mouse works with both computers.  One feature I like is you can swith between computers by hitting the scroll-lock key twice.  That starts to scan between the PC's and you can lock in the one you want by hitting the ESC key.  It also has the ability to hook up your computer sound.
