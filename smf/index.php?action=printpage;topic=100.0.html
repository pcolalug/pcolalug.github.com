Title: Mandrake, the official distro of former Star Trek actors
Post by: rburt3 on August 16, 2002, 10:30:02 am
Wil Wheaton, better known as Wesley Crusher on TV's Star Trek: The Next Generation, has converted (http://www.wilwheaton.net/) to Mandrake. Its the second entry down in his blog.  I know, I know, "who cares?" you ask.  Well, me.  I think its another great migration story to share with newbs.  

"This install was the easiest thing I have ever done, and there wasn't one single problem. The only glitch came when I was trying to get my soundcard to work, which was hammered out quickly and painlessly, thanks to the monkeys in the soapbox."

Shame about being cut from the new film, though.
