I've got Mandrake 10 Community installed and running here, and must say that I'm impressed.  Everything seems to be working flawlessly right out of the box.  Some highlights/tidbits:

Uses kernel 2.6.3, Mozilla 1.6, Gnome 2.4.2, KDE 3.2.
Mandrake Control Center has been reworked.
Menus have been reworked.
Comes on 4 CD's, kernel source is included.

Sound works, LAN works, mouse works.
Installed 2 network printers (using MCC/cups) painlessly and perfectly.
Installed latest nVidiia driver, works fine.
System seems to be very fast and snappy.

Again, I'm pretty pleased with it, although have only been using it for an hour or so.  Have not yet looked at KDE, but will soon.  The ONLY weird thing I've seen is that my eth0 interface reports initialization "failed" during bootup messages, but in fact works fine.  Gonna look into that some more.  I will have these CD's at the Installfest if anyone wants to burn a copy...
