Title: Re: Analog to Digital conversion services in Pensacola? Any recommendations?
Post by: rugmonster on February 14, 2006, 04:20:19 pm
Why not do this yourself? With a sound card and a male-to-male 1/8th" jack audio cable, you can capture audio and then it is trivial to convert said audio to mp3 or any other audio format. Video is simple as well utilizing any video capture card. Hauppauge makes some great MPEG-2 hardware encoder cards that work superbly in Linux. You can pick up a PVR-150 for less than $100 online.

Using all F/OSS tools, you can create DVD video discs with menus and the works.

If I remember correctly, Brent bought my PVR-150 and I'm sure he has a sound card. If you are really itching to pay someone, I'm sure he would take some cash to convert your stuff  :P
