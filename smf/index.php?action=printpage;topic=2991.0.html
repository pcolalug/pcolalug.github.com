Title: Linux Flash Player 9
Post by: imnes on August 31, 2006, 07:36:47 am
Our current schedule for releasing the final version of Flash Player 9 for Linux is early 2007.
http://blogs.adobe.com/penguin.swf/2006/08/basic_beta_briefing.html

What follows is a list of the APIs that the current Linux Flash Player 9 development version is using.
  • General graphics: X11
  • GUI elements (dialog boxes): GTK (will detect and use GTK1 or GTK2 -- or you can force which one you want)
  • Audio I/O: ALSA
  • Camera input: Video4Linux, API version 1 
  • Threads: POSIX threads 
  • non-HTTP Networking: BSD sockets 
  • SSL: OpenSSL 
  • IME: you know what? I don't think we've settled on this one yet... 
http://blogs.adobe.com/penguin.swf/2006/07/api_review.html
