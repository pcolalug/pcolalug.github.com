Title: Pensacola rough weather
Post by: sandspur on October 19, 2007, 07:03:59 am
Hope LUG members living in the Pensacola area  fared OK in the last two days of heavy weather.

Here is a little of what was experienced out on Pensacola Beach.  Thursday before sundown 10+ inches of rain.  Frequent short power outages.  Some damage to houses on Panferio --- notably the landmark Spaceship House. 

The automated weather station at the elementary school stopped transmitting at some point overnight.  Rain at nearby Gulf Breeze High School 9+ inches since midnight.  Looks as if we have caught up on the drought in 48 hours.

No data loss or fried hardware at my place.
