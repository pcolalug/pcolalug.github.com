Post by: danc on November 09, 2003, 07:53:08 pm
Been a while since I looked at a RH screen, but...   Look for an icon in your bottom toolbar that looks like a blank computer monitor.  When you hold your cursor over the icons, it should bring up a "tooltip" that says what it is.  Look for something called "terminal".  The terms "terminal" and/or "console" are what you are looking for.  Also click on the RedHat icon in the lower left corner and look for "terminal" or "Gnome Terminal".  Any of these will bring up a blank window, which is the command line terminal window.  Good luck and let us know how you get along.

In RH9 you can go into the Menu and look for a submenu called something like System Tools. Under that there should be a choice called Terminal. Click on Terminal.

You can also hit Ctrl-Alt-F1 to get to a console. To get back to X hit Alt-F7
