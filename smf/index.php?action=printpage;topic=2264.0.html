Title: Try before you install. Open source scripts.
Post by: dpeach on February 09, 2005, 09:17:27 am
Here is a tremendous site (http://www.opensourcecms.com) that allows you to play with php/MySQL based content management systems. Want to know how our forums work? Go to that site and try your hand for 2 hours at being your own forum administrator. Or one of the 7 other forums they have available.

Basically what it is is they have set up a system with all kinds of php and MySQL based software. They give you full access to these programs. Every 2 hours they reload everything so that when the users have everything hosed, or have hacked it by installing viruses or malicious code, it all gets a clean up.

There are over 80 different programs you can test drive. I am working with some content management software (which if I ever get it quite like I want I will do a write up of it) and was looking for some tips when I ran across this page.
