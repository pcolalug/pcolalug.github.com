Title: Swaret - Slackware
Post by: lloydr on January 29, 2004, 04:58:04 pm
I'm using swaret and dropline just fine together on a few machines.....i'd recommend not getting the pre-release one and just getting their latest stable because its less hassle....also if you want to update to slackware-current rather than just the default 9.1, then change the version to current and change all the links that say like /slackware-$version to /slackware-current...if you don't edit the links you get connection errors, which is different than the versions from the past that all you needed was change the version= to current
