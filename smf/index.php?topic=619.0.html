Finally got the 9.1 iso's downloaded from ftp.esat.net.  My problem turned out to be a setting in IE.  I had to go under the advanced Internet Options and turn on, "passive FTP."  Once I did that the downloads worked like a charm.

I found a great checksum verifier (for WinXP) at http://www.md5summer.org.

Also discovered I was burnng my iso's to CDR at too high a rate of speed.  Once I let the software determine the max burn speed, everything proceeded smoothly.

Installation went fairly smoothly.  I got only one error (error installing packages: zlib1-dvel-1.1.4-5mdk.i586).  However, after completing the install, everything seems to work fine in spite of the error.

It is so nice to have linux installed again!
