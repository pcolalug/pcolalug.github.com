Post by: imnes on November 20, 2003, 10:10:07 am
Ok if you have to setup a route to your AP then your AP is probably acting as a router.  I assume you have another router between you and your DSL / Cablemodem?  I would configure the AP to only pass data through, not to act as a router or serve DHCP info.  

That's the way I have my network, that way weather I'm on wireless or wired, I'm using the same router, and the same DHCP server and everything.
