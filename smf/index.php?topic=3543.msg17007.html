At the June meeting we started talking about doing a LAN party for the August meeting date. August normal meeting would be the 20th. It was proposed that we do a LAN party the 22nd (the following Saturday). This would be in lieu of the normal meeting. We need you to do a few things for us.

Will you make an effort to be there? Please post your interest and if it is possible for you to be there. This does not signify a full commitment at this time, but we want to make sure there is enough possible interest to do a LAN party again. The reason we stopped was they were less and less in attendance.

Will your propose a game? We are planning to have an ET (Enemy Territory) server up and running. This is our old standby game. Please plan to install and play ET. But don't practice too much, I need some chance of killing someone and surviving more than just a few minutes. But do you have another game you would like to play? Would you be willing to put together a game server for that night and let us know what game it is?

Will you tell others about the LAN party? We want to use this to get potential new members. They don't have to be running Linux to attend the party. Though they might take some verbal abuse, there will not be any broken bones. Bring along anyone you know who is even mildly interested in Linux. We would love to have them. Especially if they stink at ET.
