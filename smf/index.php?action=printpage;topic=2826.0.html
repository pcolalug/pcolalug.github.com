Title: Nvidia and kernel 2.6.16
Post by: danc on March 26, 2006, 03:45:38 pm
Decided to update a couple of machines to the new 2.6.16 kernel today.  As you may know, whenever you do this on a system using Nvidia's driver, you must also re-compile the Nvidia driver after you get the new kernel running.  Well...., there is a glitch somewhere (maybe "incompatibility" is a better word) with the driver and the 2.6.16 kernel.  Not exactly sure where the problem lies, but the normal way of using the ".run" driver installer from Nvidia will fail.  There is a patch to the driver available (below) which fixes things nicely, and am posting the link here for anyone who may be trying this and having problems like I did.  Once patched, it compiles and installs perfectly.

Here's the link:
http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=62021

Get updated!   ;)

Dan
