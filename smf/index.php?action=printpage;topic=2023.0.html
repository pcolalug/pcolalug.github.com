Title: Apache2 on SuSE 9.1
Post by: dpeach on November 12, 2004, 11:00:55 pm
There are a couple of ways to do this I would think.

One is that you could just ask here (maybe with the version of 2 that you are running) and someone could give you a copy of theirs. Or, you could get the source of Apache2 and look in the ./docs/conf directory of the tarball. There are a couple of different config files in there.

That would get you a config file. I don't know if anything else is messed up. If you suspect that you have deeper problems than just the config file, you could uninstall Apache (easy to do in Mandrake) and then make sure that it pulled your config files out. Then just reinstall that one program.

*hint* Make a backup next time. *hint* It is so easy to remember to say that to others, but I find myself getting in trouble for not doing it.
