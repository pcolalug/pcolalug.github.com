Title: 2008-2009 Steering Committee Nominations
Post by: green_dragon37 on September 03, 2008, 06:05:28 pm
It's time again to nominate Steering Committee members for the coming year.  Please post your nominations in this thread.  Be sure to include name (and/or forum name) and the position you wish to nominate this person for.  If you have multiple nominations, please include them in a single post.  If you wish to nominate yourself for any position, feel free to!

The Steering Committee is a core group of LUG members who are responsible for the day-to-day running of P'cola LUG. The positions are:

1. President - responsible for inter-organizational contacts, organizing events, and presides over meetings.  Incumbent:  Ian Barr (me)

2. Vice President - primary assistant to the President and responsible for President's duties, should the President be unavailable.   Incumbent: 

3. Secretary - responsible for intra-organizational communication, posting meeting notes, summaries, and announcements. Also responsible for forwarding/replying to email sent to info@pcolalug.org.   Incumbent: 

4. Librarian - responsible for the P'cola LUG library administration, "review book" ordering and distribution, and primary contact for O'Reilly.  Incumbent:  Brett Hoff (bhoff)

Two other positions - Webmaster and SysAdmin - are also part of the Steering Committee and are appointed by same. These folks are responsible for the maintenance of our website and hardware assets respectively.  These two positions can be held by a single person.  Incumbent (both positions):  Lloyd Randall (lloydr)
 
If you are nominated, please carefully consider your acceptance. These positions take some time, effort, and commitment on your part. It's not much, really, but please consider your other obligations. Talk to some of the current and/or former Committee members if you have specific questions about a position.  Once you have decided to accept (or decline) the nomination, please post a reply in this thread to that effect!

Nominations will be accepted from now until the 18th of September, and at the meeting itself. We will vote as a group at the September meeting. You must be present at the September meeting to vote, but you don't have to be there to win.  The new committee should have all appointed positions filled and officially assume its duties on October 1st.

Please take a minute to consider who you'd like to see elected to help our LUG function and grow, and PLEASE set aside some time to make it to the September meeting to VOTE!

Thanks,
Ian
