Hurricane Policy
« Reply #4 on: July 07, 2005, 07:23:19 pm »
Here are a few of the things I do to prepare my system for bad Weather thought I would pass it along as we all get a little frazzled when trying to get ready for something like this.
First back up any important data to cd and put this some place safe or better yet take with you...I like to keep mine in the freezer as if there is a fire I would  still have my data....
find the plugs for all your equipment and manually unplug them all be sure to cut off any apc you have. You have to manually unplug from the wall to avoid unesscary surges that can fry your equipment. next  take some plastic garbage bags and put one over your printers, towers and monitors....this does an amazing job of protecting from moisture if you lose a window or get a limb thru your roof. I have found these steps to lifesavers for my equipment.  Also a few good pictures and serial numbers never hurt maybe take them before shutting down and putting them on that back-up disk.

Brett

Everyone take care I am here for the duration.
