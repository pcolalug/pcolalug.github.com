Doesn't work for me, lloydr.

However, I've found a fix for it that /did/ work.  The refresh rate isn't determined by the game, it's controlled by your X configuration.

Open up your /etc/X11/XF86Config (or XF86Config-4, depending on your distro)

Find the section labeled "Monitor"

There should be two lines here, something that reads like the following:

Code:
   HorizSync  30-95
    VertRefresh 60-150



The lowest number determines the absolute lowest refresh rate that your monitor can handle.  Simply increase the lower boundry (don't push it past a point that your monitor cannot handle, though..) on both.  Here's what mine looks like now:

Code:
   HorizSync  50-95
    VertRefresh 85-150


Anyways, this is what works for me. I now have UT2k4 running at 85hz at 1024x768 resolution.
