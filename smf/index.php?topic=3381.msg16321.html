Meeting Day Change
« on: August 16, 2008, 10:35:59 pm »
It's been suggested that weekend meetings could be less desirable for our members.  They could interfere with social events on Saturday evenings, and they usually mean an extra trip in to town, just for us.  It has been proposed that our meeting date be changed to a weekday so that it's more convenient for people who are in town anyways, for work, to make it.  I think a day like Wednesday or Thursday would be great for our meetings, since Friday can still be a social night, and people are really just ready to get home and relax then too.

Please vote for a day, and then suggest a time of day for the meeting to occur.  Our current time of 6pm may be too early for some people who get off of work at that time.  Also, I don't think we should change which week in the month the meeting occurs, the third x day of the month seems like a good formula to keep.
