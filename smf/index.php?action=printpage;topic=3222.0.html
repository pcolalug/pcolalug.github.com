Title: Gizmo!
Post by: bhoff on September 07, 2007, 02:25:31 pm
I finally loaded a sip softphone on my Nokia 770, Choice was Gizmo found here.
http://gizmoproject.com/ (http://gizmoproject.com/) This is an excellent package and allows you to call to sip phones and in network phones without charge. they have clients for mac/windows/and Linux and for most mobile paltforms.
You can opt up for call out minutes to any land line or cell phone for as little as 10.oo at a time calls are 1.9c a minute.
I have used it around the area calling office and works rather well with Nokia and all I need is a wifi connection.
Let me know if anyone uses it and I will share contacts so we can try out all the features.
