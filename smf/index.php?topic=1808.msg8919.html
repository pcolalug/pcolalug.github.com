OK, well, I have the box in my hot hands...  First of all, there's been a lot of hype about the hardware requirements for this game.  Here's what the box itself says is the minimums required:

Pentium4 1.5Ghz  or  AthlonXP 1500+
64Mb 3D accelerated video card
384Mb RAM
2.2Gb HD space
8x CDROM
DirectX9.0b compatible sound card
Microsoft Windows XP or 2000

The box specifically says that 95/98/ME/NT is not supported, and there is no mention of Linux.  I looked around on the CD's (there's 3 of them) and there's no sign of a Linux installer (hopefully there will be one out soon).

I got a message from tux games about it:

Quote

Dear Tux Games customer.

As you have pre-ordered Doom 3 from us
we are writing to update you on its progress and to ask you
to confirm information to us.

Doom 3 was released in North America earlier this week. Our
stock comes from the UK and the UK release is due in the next
few days.

Currently, there is NO Linux client. It was not included in
the retail box, and will be made available later.

As we are a Linux game company, we plan to hold off on
shipping until we are able to include a full Linux installer.

However we are aware that some of you may wish to receive
your copies earlier, and so we will ship copies without the
Linux installer to anyone that requests it. Simply reply to
this email and let us know you would like us to ship as soon
as the game is released

Additionally, we now know that there will be two versions of
the game, one on CD, one  on DVD. We are by default assigning
all preorders to be the CD version as that will be the safer
option. If you would like to have your order swapped to the
DVD version, please just reply to this email and let us know.

Finally, if you have changed address in the time since your
preorder, please let us know where to ship the game to.

Thanks

Tux Games Sales
