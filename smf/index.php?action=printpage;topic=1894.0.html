Title: After Ivan
Post by: sandspur on October 02, 2004, 09:14:13 am
Hola Penguinistas---Just to give a status report.  My little block house on Pensacola Beach is in amazingly good condition.  It had chest-high water and the contents (including my two computers) are completely trashed.  The place is filthy but I can deal with that.

Authorities are restricting access to the Island and it is still under martial law.  This is actually a good thing, although inconvenient for me, because there is still a lot of sand to be cleared off roads by heavy equipment and Gulf Power has a lot of work to do with power lines.

This Sunday residents will be allowed on the Island 08:00-17:00.  I'll be toting nasty things out to the street trying to clear the house of all my stuff.

In good spirits but for sure a space cadet from the aftermath of Ivan.

If I ever get back in the house with electricity and my DSL I'm going to have built a Linux box on steroids.  I evacuated with my FC2 and PGP disks.
