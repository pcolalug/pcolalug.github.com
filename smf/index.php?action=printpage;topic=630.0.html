Title: Game suggestions per specs
Post by: rburt3 on April 18, 2003, 08:29:23 am
Fix your TV and revisit Final Fantasy X!

Really, though, I had a great time with the ERA linux demo for all of the 25 minutes I have a month to play games. It was originally concieved as a re-make of the Ultima series of games, but is now more of an omage, if you will (and you might!). Not a lot to do yet, but it looks pretty. I can't remember the url off hand, but you're sure to find it on the Linux Game Tome (http://www.happypenguin.org).

You should also give the Neverwinter Nights beta a try.

As for your upgrades, they'll probably need to be done before you give UT a try. I would sugguest that if money is tight, go with one of the GeForce 3 Ti graphics cards. They have plenty of power and run today's games fabulously. The N-Force based mobos aren't too bad, either, from what I've heard. You could probably do all of this (card, mobo, processor) for a couple hundred (assuming you used last-gen parts and went with AMD, that is).
