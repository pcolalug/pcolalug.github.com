Saw this post as I was searching for some Xgl info on SLED 10.
I happen to work for Novell and happen to be originally from Pensacola...figured I'd chime in on the question. Unfortunately I don't get back there very often, but would like to have a reason to...

SLED 10 will be shipped most likely in August. Don't have official pricing, but currently NLD9 is $50/user. OpenSUSE is our opensourced equivalent and while it won't have anything that is licensed, it will have most of the goodies and you can download it from opensuse.org at no cost. You can also download the latest beta builds of SUSE 10.1 from there (basis for SLED10). We're currently at beta9 with roughly 3-4 more before going into release candidates in early May. Beta 9 looks good and each released build is making significant progress.

Hope that helps.
