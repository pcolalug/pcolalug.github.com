Title: Linux Install Fest
Post by: imnes on February 27, 2005, 01:10:08 pm
We are having our Linux Install Fest on Saturday March 19 from 2pm to 7pm.  This is a chance for anyone interested in learning about Linux to come by and get help installing and using it on your computer.  

The Install Fest will be at All Pro Sound, they are located at 806 Beverly Pkwy in Pensacola.  Please use the rear parking lot.

Download a copy of our official flyer. (http://pensacola-linux.com/lug/plug_flyer_updated2.pdf)  You are encouraged to copy and pass it around.

Click here for a map and driving directions. (http://www.mapquest.com/maps/map.adp?searchtype=address&country=US&addtohistory=&searchtab=home&address=806+Beverly+Parkway&city=Pensacola&state=FL&zipcode=)
