Title: IBM $300M Linux Ad Campaign
Post by: imnes on April 03, 2006, 08:14:28 am
According to Larry D'Agostino (http://linuxblogger.dyndns.org/weblog/index.php?/archives/162-Linux-gets-a-big-media-boost-from-IBM.html)
Quote
Tech News World is reporting that IBM will spend $300 million on an ad campaign to challenge Microsoft enterprise products. One of the features of the advertising campaign is the "Migrate to the Penguin." IBM will give rebates to software resellers who are able to move customers from Windows to Linux.

This is an amazing move by a big player, perhaps the biggest player in software. Linux has never seen so much popularity. We should see a lot of press in the next coming months. Be prepared to talk around the water cooler about Linux.
