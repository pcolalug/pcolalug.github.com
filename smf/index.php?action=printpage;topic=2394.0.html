Title: Book review: Linux Server Hacks
Post by: erc on April 20, 2005, 12:53:16 am
I went out and bought this book, I didn't get it from the group library.  If you don't have it, you need to get it in your hot little hands RIGHT NOW.  Hack #65 (quick logins with ssh client keys), hack #82 (setting up replication in MySQL), and hack #93 (generating an ssl certificate and signing request) make it well worth the price of admission, but this book is chock full of all kinds of tips and tricks that I can't keep in my head.  Every time I browse through it I find another tip or trick I can use.  This isn't one of those "Advanced Linux" books that wind up being a rehash of someone else's beginner book, this is the Real Deal, kids!  Hey, maybe you can keep all this stuff in your head, but I can't ;)

Browse it at B&N or Books-a-Million and you'll see what I mean.  It's well worth a look :)
