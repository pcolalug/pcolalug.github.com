Title: Macpup Linux 061
Post by: djoed_pcola on October 02, 2009, 10:39:33 pm
Macpup 061 is a very nice remaster of Puppy Linux with the Enlightenment window manager.  I recently downloaded the Macpup iso and burned to a CD.  It's about a 180 MB download.  It ran beautifully on an ancient PIII 450 Mhz computer with 320 Meg of memory.  I may also try it out on an old PII 233 machine.

Here is a link to a youtube video of Macpup in action.
