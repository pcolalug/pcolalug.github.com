Title: SMF and Changing Passwords
Post by: rugmonster on December 30, 2005, 10:20:39 am
Greetings all! I wanted to pass along an issue and workaround I discovered with our forum. I recently changed my password (it's a good thing to do every now and again), and when I went to log in again, it gave me an error about my cookies. To log back in, I had to go through my cookie cache and delete the cookies for pcolalug.org. I had to do that for every computer I had logged in with.

Steps for Firefox 1.5 (Linux):

1. Open Edit -> Preferences

Steps for Firefox 1.5 (Windows):

1. Open Tools -> Options

Combined Steps for Firefox 1.5:

2. Select the "Privacy" section
3. Click on the "Cookies" tab
4. Click on "View Cookies"
5. In the search box at top of window, type in pcolalug
6. Delete all pcolalug.org cookies
7. Log in

Other browsers should be similar. All you need to do is delete the cookies associated with pcolalug.org and you'll be good to go.

Cheers! ;D
