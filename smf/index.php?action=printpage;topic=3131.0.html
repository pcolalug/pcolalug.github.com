Job Title:  Senior Technical Support Engineer (Pensacola, FL) 
Position Type:  Full Time, Regular
 
Business Unit Area/Functional Area:  Information Tech
 
Country:  USA
 
State/Province:  Florida
 
City:  Pensacola
 
Relocation Approved:  No
 
Job Description:  This position is responsible for the enhancements and daily operations of the Clearwire Contact Center's network infrastructure. The successful candidate will have proven experience in implementing and maintaining a call center voice and data networks with a primary focus on the Clearwire Avaya voice platform. This person will work very closely with the customer care and other IT teams to meet aggressive deployment and performance objectives. This position requires a highly motivated individual comfortable with rapid changes in direction and environment who is passionate about delivery. The successful candidate will customer-oriented, results driven and passionate about building a cost efficient and flexible telecommunications infrastructure.

(40%) Partner and work closely with the customer care, Avaya, vendors, and other IT teams to rapidly deliver enhancements to our Avaya platform. Ensure improvements to the platform are implemented in a timely basis with little to no interruption to ongoing business operations. Consistently demonstrate strong ownership for ability for platform to meet business needs.

(20%) Ensure Avaya platform and voice network meet acceptable uptime, performance and capacity requirements. Passionately pursue operational and customer service excellence with a bias towards data and metrics based decision making. Demonstrate a strong ownership to learn and improve Avaya and other technical skills as required. and have an aptitude for teaching less experienced team members.

(20%) Communicate frequently regarding project implementation and platform operation status to team mates. Share concerns about issues and ideas for resolution. Demonstrated ability for working unsupervised and independently to meet business objectives.

(10%) Demonstrated ability to work well in team environments. Share technical knowledge and teach less experienced team members so they can improve their skills.

(10%) Ensure we are being cost-efficient. Help manage vendors and contractors to get great value. Spend Clearwire money as if it was your own.


Position Requirements

5+ years experience with technical support of enterprise call center operations
3+ years experience with Avaya platforms (Avaya Certification required - CMS, CTI, VOIP)
3+ years experience with IVR implementation and lifecycle management
3+ years experience with network operations
1+ years vendor management experience
1+ years prior start-up experience

The successful candidate will have the following characteristics:

-Degree in Computer Science or engineering and/or 3+ years related work experience
-Ability to efficiently orchestrate and coordinate vendor escalations as required
-Flexibility - we work in a very fluid and dynamic environment - ability to adapt to rapid changes in direction is crucial
-Teamwork - ability to work effectively and build the relationships with both the IT and Care leadership teams
-Work ethic - willingness and enthusiasm to work non-standard hours as needed to get the job done and to meet the needs of our customers
-Ability to learn new things quickly and inquisitive enough to challenge what does not make sense
-Excellent communication, troubleshooting and analytical skills
-Good working knowledge of IP Telephony, Linux and Telecom and Networking protocols
-Demonstrated track record for cost reduction
-Religious/passionate orientation and attention to customers and solution delivery
-Ability to thrive in a dynamic and challenging work environment

Preferred Qualifications:

1+ years experience with a wireless service provider
3+ years experience with multiple call center environments and operations
CCNA certification
Experience with Microsoft desktop administration
Experience supporting Email/Chat integration on Avaya platform
Experience with ordering, testing voice circuits
