Ok, this is kinda way off topic, but that's why I put it here under Misc.

We started advertising a new service at BestBuy this weekend called Rhapsody. It's a music streaming service. Well, being the geek I am, I had to go home and try it out.

This thing is cool! Unfortunately, it only runs on Windoze for now.

In a nutshell, It gives you access to over 25,000 albums on line. You can create custom playlists and swap them between friends. It's all CD quality.
I signed up for the trial last night and couldn't believe the variety of stuff. You can also take tracks and burn them to CD.

It's nice to be able to do this legally....

Anyway, I liked it so much, I had to let you all know about it.

You can get to it through here or here. The first link gives you a 14 day free trial while the second is only a 7 day free trial.

PS. This is not an AD for BestBuy. I just thought it was pretty cool and geeky. That's why I included the second link.

If anyone has any questions about it, let me know. I'm pretty sure I'm gonna subscribe once my trial is up.
