September 2009 Meeting
« on: September 17, 2009, 08:35:15 pm »
We had a good meeting tonight with 5 in attendance. We talked a bit about the elections coming up next month and a few other club topics.

Mainly we talked about the NX Free software. While a few at the meeting already have Log-Me-In and Go To Meeting accounts, the combination of Hamachi and NX Free accomplish the same task for Linux. (Hamachi is now owned by Log-Me-In). I was very interested in this presentation by Ed since it particularly fulfills a need that I have in my own network.

While we struggled a bit to show the full power of the software over the Internet (because of firewall restrictions), Ed did a good job of getting it set up quickly to show on the local network. Thanks Ed.

Have any decisions been made yet on this?  The reason I ask is because it seems quite clear that the post should have been made on September 1 (per the bylaws).  In nearly all cases, elections have been held in September, with very few exceptions.  The last 2 years, it had been postponed either by those at the meeting in September, or due to lack of nominations for a position that has to be filled.  The latest that a nominations post has ever been made (even when elections occurred in October), is September 24.

Looking through the forums, we can see when things have been posted:

2003-2004 - September 10 (see http://www.pcolalug.org/smf/index.php?topic=994.0), election held in September 2003
2004-2005 - August 6 (see http://www.pcolalug.org/smf/index.php?topic=1814.0), election held in October 2004
2005-2006 - August 25 (see http://www.pcolalug.org/smf/index.php?topic=2570.0), election held in September 2005
