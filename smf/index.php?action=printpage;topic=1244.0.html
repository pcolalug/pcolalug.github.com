Ed,

I don't know if you guys got this resolved our just put it on the back burner, but I just got done setting up a VPN with a friend across town. Using my Linksys WRT54G (http://www.linksys.com/products/product.asp?grid=33&scid=35&prid=601) and a firmware (http://ftp://ftp.sveasoft.com/pub/Satori_v2_2.00.8.7sv-pre1.bin.zip) from Sveasoft (http://www.sveasoft.com/), I was able to make a PPTP server. At his house, he has setup VPN connections on his Windows machines to "dial in" to my server and his systems are now part of the VPN. It was very easy to setup. As soon as I get my domain controller setup, we'll be sharing a domain for authentication. Supposedly, the next version of the firmware hopes to have IPSec included, so we will move to that as soon as it becomes available.

If you would like further details, the Sveasoft forums (http://www.sveasoft.com/modules/phpBB2/index.php) helped me out a lot, or PM me and I would be more than happy to give you a call and lend any assistance I can.
