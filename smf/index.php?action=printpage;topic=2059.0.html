Nobody said it was going to cease to exist.  However, Slackware *as we know it* is /certainly/ in question.

Quote from: Rugmonster

There are certainly enough people who know Slackware Linux well, yes.  But since Slackware Linux is essentially a one-man-show, if he disappears, so does Slackware.  Of course it will continue, under some other name/method.
