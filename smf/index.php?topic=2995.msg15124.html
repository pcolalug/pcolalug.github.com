You have to have a motherboard that supports dual power supplies and typically, you won't get that outside of enterprise class workstations and servers. Dual power supplies are only worth it when your system needs high reliability. A home system rarely qualifies as that.

If you have been having this problem with quality power supplies in multiple locations, I would venture to say your motherboard may be the root of the problem. I still think a line conditioner would do you a lot of good to deal with power spikes and brown outs. Both can be detrimental to any and all of your hardware.
