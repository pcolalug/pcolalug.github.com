Title: Digital Life TV
Post by: dpeach on September 17, 2005, 10:53:44 pm
www.digitallifetv.com is a new program that is being funded by Ziff-Davis (of tech publishing fame). It is a Internet streamed TV program with commercials and all (which is why you should wait till it is over and download it). I am not sure if they have plans to make it available on networks or just keep it Internet.

This "digital life" site is much more than the TV program, but that is all that I have really looked into.

Patrick Norton from The Screen Savers is the host. Robert Herron is his co-host. Robert is a tech dude at ZD Publishing.

The show is live on Tuesday nights. There are several download formats to get the show afterwards.

This is a new show and it is pretty cheap looking at the moment. They are in the process of building the studio, but are broadcasting from there. So it is kinda neat to see the backdrop change each week.
