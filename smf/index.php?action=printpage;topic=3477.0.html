Title: Interesting Unix time
Post by: danc on February 13, 2009, 03:07:01 pm
Later this afternoon, an interesting event will happen...  At time 17:31:30 (CST) today, the "Unix time" will be:  1234567890 .  For those who don't know, Unix time started counting (by seconds) on January 1st, 1970.  Unix time is also known as "Epoch time".

Some nifty tricks:

You can watch this occur in a terminal, with this command:

watch -n 1 date +%s

Or, if you're CLI-challenged, watch it unfold here:

http://coolepochcountdown.com/

Good stuff!
