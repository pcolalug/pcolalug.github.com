Linspire has integrated their SIPphone (IP Based phone) service into gaim and named it PhoneGaim.  As usual, linspire uses get a binary package and other users can grab the source.  Their SIP phone service is free, you get a phone number, can call other IP based phone users for free, and can call regular phones.  I think the site says you get 5 minutes of calls to 10 different countries free per day.

http://www.homeweb.org/~imnes/linux/linspire/screens/phonegaim1.png

The website for their SIP phone service is http://www.sipphone.com/
They have physical phone hardware, and software-based phones for Windows, Mac, and Linux.

And the website for PhoneGaim is
http://www.phonegaim.com/
