Post by: dpeach on February 05, 2005, 11:56:38 am
Here is a thread (http://www.pcolalug.org/phpBB2/viewtopic.php?t=2244) that was recently discussed. You can go through the steps Jaymz did to determine sound card type and we could see if you have the right driver loaded.

Go through the steps listed there:
Code:
lspci
cat /proc/asound/cards
lsmod

Each one of those is a separate step (and ran as root) and you can post your output to the forum here with each step so we can possibly help.

Jamyz' fix was ultimately to turn off "External Amp" inside of KMix. But I assume you are using Gnome as your window manager. I don't know what to look for there.
