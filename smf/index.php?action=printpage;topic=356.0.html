Post by: crowe on January 07, 2003, 09:26:54 am
I finally managed to do it!  I got gentoo to build on a 486/33SX w/ 20MB RAM.

Of course, this did come at a cost, ie, besides having to leave the machine on for a week and half straight throughout the entire build, I also had to build a boot/root floppy for this specific machine.  That proved a bit of a hassle mostly in the way of having to actually build the disk from scratch.. turns out that this specific machine was very non-standard and rather quirksome in many, many ways.

But the end result was quite nice, given the crufty old box it's now running on.  I had to forgo many of the things such as having a GUI, etc.

And now why did I do this? Because I'm a very studly geek.  Oh yes.
