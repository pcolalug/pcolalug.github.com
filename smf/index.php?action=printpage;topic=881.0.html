My understanding of the library is this:

There are books that belong to the group (those registered to user 'librarian' in the library system).  danc is in charge of those, and holds them in his possesion on behalf of the group.

The other books (those registered to anyone else other than 'librarian') are owned by that person.   The whole system works on a lending-library concept -- the books still belong to (and reside with) whoever owns them, since they are merely offering to make them available.
