Title: agedu -- How old are the files on your drive?
Post by: dpeach on May 26, 2009, 11:23:40 am
I read about a new software that digs around your hard drive to evaluate the age of the files on your machine. It is called agedu. I installed it and am working to figure it out now.

The idea is that you may have files that you have not accessed in years that are taking up space that you can dump to a backup and delete from the working drive. If you are tight on space this can be a huge help. If you have unlimited resources for storage, then this might not be as helpful for you.

http://www.chiark.greenend.org.uk/~sgtatham/agedu/

It takes just a bit of reading that page above and the --help option to learn how to use it.
