Title: Installation on a laptop
Post by: sandspur on May 31, 2010, 10:47:54 am
Installed the live ISO version from a disk.  A good basic system but, for a good reason, short on a big selection of applications.

Built-in wireless worked fine.

Since hard drive space is not generally an issue on newer laptops or PCs I recommend getting the DVD-ISO (instead of the live version) and installing it for a better assortment of applications.  Get it here:

http://fedoraproject.org/en/get-fedora-options#formats
