Title: New Project to fill my Linux night
Post by: bhoff on December 17, 2005, 08:04:46 pm
I started with an lcd projector screen that I bought some time ago for the overhead projector project. It is only black and white and 640x480 nothing to fancy.
I had grown board of it's limited ability some time ago and it has been collecting dust. Well now it has a new life ....I first ripped the whole thing down to it's bare components and ended up with an lcd with a nice little plastic frame for mounting and the associated electronics on one board. One problem I had is there was a limit of then about 4 inches due to a small 16 conductor flat cable. I pulled it loose and desoldered the edge connectors paying attention to the pin numbering on each end. I  then soldered two pieces of 8 conductor cable about 2.5 feet long between the controls and the lcd screen.....My first attempt was a success.....
I then hung the lcd over my 21"monitor (I plan on buying a flex arm for it later or mounting into the side of my computer) anyway I now have an interesting overlay screen that I have a terminal window that I can see thru but look at also to see data or work on it.....Is actually pretty cool I think the controls give me pretty good control over the lcd and even has a clear button that I can hit to see thru the screen without any overlay....maybe I will get a camera for christmas and see if loyd can post some pictures....
