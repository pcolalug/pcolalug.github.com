Title: Re: New Favorite Song
Post by: rugmonster on January 18, 2007, 07:39:56 am
ah kids these days...

mpeg is a non-compressed format. The audio you're used to in mp3, wma, or ogg are compressed, lossy formats. Yeah, you may get 5 minutes of music in 5 megs, but you're losing a little bit of quality. Granted, the gains in reduced disk space outweigh the losses in quality for most people, if you have a good audio setup, you'll be able to tell the difference.
