Quote from: imnes
Installed SuSE 9.2 to try some hardware out with it, and I thought I was stuck in 800x600 resolution.  But it was actually my usual 1600x1200, but SuSE (or kde) resized everything to take up a lot more screen space than I want.  Anybody know how to scale it all down?


To begin with, right click on the task bar at the bottom and set size to "small".

Next go to "Control Center" in the "K" menu and then click on "Appearance & Themes" You can play with "Fonts" and "Icons" in there to control the KDE system defaults.
