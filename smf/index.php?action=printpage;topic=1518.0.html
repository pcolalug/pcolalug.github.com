Title: Need help with supermount on slackware 9.1
Post by: rugmonster on April 01, 2004, 05:23:24 pm
Supermount is like automounter. You can setup supermount to handle your removable media such as floppies or cd-roms. Rather than having to mount and unmount a filesystem everytime it is changed, you can just insert your cd-rom or floppy and access the directory. Supermount handles mounting the media and unmount it when it is no longer needed. It's just one of those things that makes sense for certain media types.
