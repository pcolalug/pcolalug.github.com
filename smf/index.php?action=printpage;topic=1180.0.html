Title: Mandy 9.1 and Printers
Post by: on November 25, 2003, 12:24:06 pm
Hi David, I don't have to do that each time. Mine are always there "sticky" as you say. I didn't have to do anything special my printers are on a print server and I use CUPS. I've never set a local printer but in my case I have had to configure CUPS (by clicking on that option in MCC) and do a refresh for it to find them but after it did and I clicked DONE everything worked. You can also do some configurating of printers in KDE control center under Preipherals > Printers using Administrator Mode.
