Training Sales Representative

 

GBSI believes that workforce retention is dependent upon helping the work force to achieve and retain industry recognized certifications and quality training opportunities. Our successful training operations are causing us to increase our staff with a Sales Representative to help support our effort to grow our presence in the Gulf Coast and eventually Nationwide. GBSI specializes in IT training; however, we also provide management, quality, and general education courses.

Ideal candidates will provide commercial business to business training sales in the Pensacola and the Gulf Coast area.  Candidates should have excellent verbal and written communication skills, a strong desire to succeed, and superior time management skills.

 Essential Job Functions

Make local business contacts who need technical training and build professional relationships.
Communicate client requests and scheduled events with Training coordination staff.
Use client feedback to help create a schedule that meets the needs of the local area.
Provide informed sales pitches to local businesses, emphasizing the benefits of training their staff.
Provide customers information on additional services (catering, certification testing, room rentals)
Provide all documentation and reports required by management on time.
Basic Qualifications:

Some sales experience and knowledge of IT subjects/certifications a plus.

Friendly and customer service oriented professional willing to assist in a wide variety of ways. Must be a self starter able to work independently and as part of a group.

Must be a proficient user of MS Office products including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.

Must have reliable transportation.

Applies systems analysis techniques and procedures to enhance the overall IT system. Applies established procedures where the nature of system, feasibility, computer equipment, and programming language are predefined. Coordinates work with programmers. Develops simple to moderate ad hoc queries with assistance as required. Uses COTS software in the performance of duties. Assists in designing and developing user training materials. Assists in providing user training at various locations.

 

Basic Qualifications:

Prefer Bachelor’s with three (3) years related experience or seven (7) years in lieu with H.S. Diploma.

 

Information Assurance (IA), Technology (IT), and system support community with varying levels of expertise and knowledge including information security (INFOSEC); security software and data products; INFOSEC Systems Configuration Management; cyber cryptography; security systems architecture and design; networking, and/or hacking. Experience with desktop computers and software for word processing and database management.  Able to communicate effectively with individuals, using a high degree of tact and diplomacy.  Able to stoop, climb and lift at least 50 lbs and can load and unload trucks for computer/monitor movement and delivery to various locations. 

 

Security +, CompTia A+ and/or Network + certifications are preferred.

 

Skills: MS Office, IT Security, Information Assurance, DoD and DoN Instructions, INFOSEC

 

Clearance: Possess or be able to obtain and maintain a SECRET Clearance.
