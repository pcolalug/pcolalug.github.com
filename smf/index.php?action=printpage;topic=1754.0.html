Title: Support Mailing Lists
Post by: steve on July 06, 2004, 09:46:43 pm
One good source of support on many distros is the community mailing lists. These are mailing list discussions on specific distros and applications with support provided by the community. I've found I get better support from regular people running the same stuff as me alot of times than from the manufacturer themselves. The manufacturer, many times, doesn't have a real world setup that you/we may be using at home while other users may.

Anyway, i'm going to post links to the web pages for subscrbing to the mailing lists for my main products. Others are welcome to add to this list.

Many of these lists originate on the manufacturers web site but you'll find most of the support is provided by regular users like us.

SuSe Linux (http://www.suse.com/us/private/support/online_help/mailinglists/index.html)

Mandrake Linux (http://www.mandrakelinux.com/en/flists.php3)

Ximian Evolution and other Ximian Products (http://developer.ximian.com/community/lists.html)

These are just a few. I'll add more as I run across them.
BTW, Many of them have archives of past messages that you can read on-line.
