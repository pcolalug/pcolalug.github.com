Title: How does one "join" the group meetings"
Post by: steve on December 05, 2002, 07:59:25 pm
Wally,

This is probably a good place to start. We have meetings each month at Books-A-Million on Davis. We are talking about having an "Install Fest" sometime early next year. That's where we get together and help newbies install Linux on their computers. I'm sure people will also bombard you with some good links for getting started with Linux.

By registering on this forum and attending a couple of meetings, you automatically become a member. There aren't any dues or any cash that you need for the group. When we need money for something, which is very seldom, we ask for cash contributions.

Anyway, welcome aboard. Just keep checking back here for more info.

Steve
