Quote
Hi David,
I want to thank you for your email message. All of your members and others from your community are invited to attend our Computer Expo '09 on Saturday,
January 24, 2009, Northwest Florida State College; Niceville, FL The event is free and open to the public. This is our third computer expo and this one will be the best ever with more workshops and more vendors.

We have more than 22 workshops scheduled from 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM. Our presenters and vendors are very knowledgeable and experienced. You are more than welcomed to have someone come and setup a table (we provide tables for free) and give out handouts and have a laptop to demo the software. If they elect to attend workshops, they can leave the handouts on the table but try to be present from 12:00 noon to 1:00 PM. They can bring some copies of Kubuntu and add them to the door prizes to help get some recognition for the Pensacola Linux User's Group.

We have one more room available if you have a person who would like to make a presentation on Ubuntu or Kubuntu and the related open source software. The time slot we have available is from 9:45 AM to 10:45 AM Bldg K, Room 151. The room comes with a projector and screen. Let me know as soon as possible to get the name and brief description added to the schedule of events.

...The drive from Pensacola on Interstate I10 down Highway 85 to the college is about one hour....

Many thanks and hope to see at least 25 from your club in attendance.

I will contact him and let him know that we definitely want a table to set up a station with fliers. Do you have something already printed up mall0ry? Do you want to work with the flier we had from one of our previous events?

Who else can go?

Can anyone put together a presentation that would make us all proud? I would suggest something newbie friendly. We don't want to scare them off. It could be advanced stuff too as long as there is some pretty good WOW! factor built in. Give them a reason to have an interest in Linux.
