Title: Firefox Font in Gnome for SuSE 9.3
Post by: rugmonster on May 09, 2005, 08:44:13 pm
I found a nice little tip for helping the fonts for Firefox mesh with the rest of your Gnome environment in the heavily KDE based SuSE 9.3 desktop environment.

If you don't have .gtkrc-2.0 in your home directory, create one
Code:
$ touch .gtkrc-2.0

If it exists, you can skip that step. Now add a line to your .gtkrc-2.0 for your preferred font. Since I use Sans with a font size of 9, I added this line
Code:
gtk-font-name = "Sans 9"

With a quick restart of Firefox, I now have a nice, consistent environment.
