Title: Re: Cheap video cards benchmarked!
Post by: rugmonster on May 15, 2006, 11:24:21 pm
They serve a purpose for those who have a limited budget and need something to start off with. I am thinking of the college student who may not have a whole lot of money, needs a system to do their homework/research on, but would like to casually play a game on the weekend. These cards seem to be up to the task. They won't produce top performance, but they will get the job done. You can't complain about the price for performance. When you've got less than $1000 to put together a system, these make for great options, if you ask me.
