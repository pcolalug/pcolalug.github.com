Title: Custom Live CD
Post by: Yossarian on May 17, 2009, 03:18:12 pm
Found a nifty little website that makes a custom live CD based on Nimblex.

http://custom.nimblex.net/

Comes with a standard KDE 3.5 desktop and you pick and choose what you want.  Includes some restricted media codecs, so, I was able to play the WMA files on my computer with Amarok on it.

Rather nifty.  Works quite well with Unetbootin', so if you want a good live distro for a ~512 mb flash drive (everything I needed turned out less than 400 mb) you might look into this.
