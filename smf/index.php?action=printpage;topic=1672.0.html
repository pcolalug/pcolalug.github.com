Title: ATI Support
Post by: rugmonster on June 01, 2004, 11:35:21 am
I was talking with someone at the last meeting about the support for the ATI 9800 series cards. I haven't tried them, but every card series from 8500 to 9800 is supported by their drivers posted on the ATI site (http://www.ati.com/support/drivers/linux/radeon-linux.html?type=linux&prodType=graphic&prod=productsLINUXdriver&submit.x=10&submit.y=10&submit=GO%21) for XFree86 versions 4.1 through 4.3. The mobility drivers don't exist because ATI doesn't release standardized mobility drivers since they allow the manufacturers to modify as they see necessary unlike nvidia. Anyway, any modern ATI should work with your modern XFree86. When I get back to Oklahoma, I two systems with 9800 series cards that are getting the Gentoo treatment. I'll update once they're up and report any problems, if any.
