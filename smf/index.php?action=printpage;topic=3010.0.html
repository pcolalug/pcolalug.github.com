Title: Penetration tester's review
Post by: bhoff on September 22, 2006, 02:30:16 pm
Penetration Tester's
Open Source Toolkit

Syngress
ISBN 1-59749-021-0

Well finally the first book I have run across that is a must read!
If you administer a network or even a single server,
Heck even for your own protection.
This is the one shop stop for learning how to hack successfully!
Anybody with a computer could read this book and tear up your network.
The book is well put together and walks you thru step by step. I read it and was shaking at the implications for the security field, And even more it gives you a live distro CD with all the hacker tools on it......
Yes I know we have to know how they did it to defend against it, But this book just opened up a whole can kick butt!

I was glued to every chapter and even went as far to try the wifi attack with great success on my own network that I felt was atleast moderatly safe.

Get it today before it is to late
