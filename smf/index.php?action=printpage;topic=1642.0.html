Title: Knoppix 3.4
Post by: danc on May 20, 2004, 09:25:50 pm
Just tried out the new Knoppix 3.4 (2004-05-17 version), and must say that I am impressed (again).  This is one smooth distro.  Everything "just works".  It comes with a 2.6.6 kernel and a truly amazing amount of software for just one CD.

I am seriously thinking about doing a hard drive install of this and flex it a little.  Once it's installed you can do "apt-get update" and update everything, as it's based on Debian.  There is a new HD installer script included in this version.

I'm thinking this might even be a way for me to overcome my KDE-phobia...   :lol:
