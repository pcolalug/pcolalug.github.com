Okay, to get a standard looking setup (with just the menu, taskbar, etc on the bottom of the screen).

1.  Right-click on the bottom bar, and select "Add to Panel -> Gnome Menu"
2.  Click & Hold middle mouse button on gnome menu, and move it to the left side of the bottom bar.
3.  Click & Hold middle button to move task bar on the bottom to the right side.
4.  Right-click on the bottom bar, and select "Add to Panel -> Accessories -> Clock"
5.  Click & Hold middle button to move clock to right side.
... Keep doing this to add whatever functionality you want to the bottom.
6.  Then, right click on an empty area of the top menu bar, and select "Delete this Panel" (I think it calls it the panel, but whatever Delete option it is).  You will get a warning about loosing all it's settings, just click Okay.
7.  Logout and make sure to "Save Current Setup".  Now you should be setup as normal.

Hope this is helpful to somebody.
