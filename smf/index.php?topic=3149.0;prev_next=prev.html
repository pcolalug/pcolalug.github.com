Well guys I am back in the swing of things again after surgery and all!
Well I have acquired a few new toys the first being a Nokia n770 http://europe.nokia.com/A4144786.
I have been busy with this one as I have been looking for a portable Pentesting device.
This seems to be the ideal solution....I have also ordered a blue tooth keyboard and gps for it and plan on getting
a 2gb mmc-mobile card also....I have been busy..busy...busy getting the apps I wanted loaded on it...
Nmap, Kismet, tcpdump, ping, traceroute, aircrack-ptw, and of course metasploit.
I also installed ruby and gems with rails for metasploit. wish I could find a ruby-ftk2 and ruby-libglade2 deb also
But mfsweb and mfsconsole work..with mfsconsole being my poison of choice.
Kismet works really well.....I had read about false reading and have not had that problem yet.

I also installed xchat, vnc-viewer, vim, and bash to boot.....for them slow times I loaded DOOM also.
Got to have atleast one game.

Update installing a bluetooth sniffer as I write this since this device has bluetooth and wifi built in.
Plan on having it at this months meeting,

My second Toy was a new book hacking the wrt54g. http://www.amazon.com/Linksys-WRT54G-Ultimate-Hacking-Asadoorian/dp/1597491667
This is a great book all about hacking all the models of the wrt54g, wrtsl54gs and wrt54gs.
I plan on printing out the section on adding sd card to your router...for general talk at next meeting.

Anyway that is what I have been up to while I was recuperating.....Hope to see you all at the meeting.
