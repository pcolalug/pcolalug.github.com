Well, I downloaded macpup Linux and tried it out as a Live-CD on old and newer computers.  I also installed it on an old 256 MB bootable USB drive.  Macpup is a remaster of Puppy Linux but with quite a bit more "out of the box" polish.  Anyway, you might want to try it out for yourself.  Macpup does request a 2 dollar donation.  All I can say is that it is the best 2 bucks I ever spent.
