David,
TS is just a method where you can talk with other players.  Just install the TS client (http://www.teamspeak.org).  Yes, you need a mic and headphones/speakers (the combo headsets work nicely).  Then you just fire up the TS client, get a list of servers from the web, and join the one you want.  Very easy and it'll be obvious when you see it.

And if you can't make TS work, you can still play just fine without it.
