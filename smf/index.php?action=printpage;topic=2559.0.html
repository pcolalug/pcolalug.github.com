Title: avidemux
Post by: Brent on August 20, 2005, 02:30:04 pm
Hi all

I've been looking for a good linux video editor for a while now.  Video editing is one area where, i'm sad to say, linux hasn't quite caught up with windows commercial applications across the board.  I've tried just about every video editor out there wihout much sucess.  Most linux video editors don't support editing mpgs, which is the native format of my pvr 250, not to mention the mp4 avi format I had already encoded most of my video into to save space.

I recently reinstalled freerock gnome.  Among the menus I found Avidemux.  It's a simple mp4 video editor.  I'll say that again, it's a video editor which natively supports the codec which I've encoded most of my video!  I'm still learning the ins and outs of the software, but so far it looks to be everything i've been looking for in a video editor.  Honestly, it's the best one I've seen yet for windows or linux.

http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/

Heck, if this pans out I might even be able to do a presentation on video capture/editing/encoding in linux.

Brent
