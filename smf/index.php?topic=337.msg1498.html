I have been more than pleased with Phoenix. I downloaded the newest Alpha of Mozilla and have only fired it up once to see what a particular page looked like in the two browsers (exactly the same). I like the small and fast of Phoenix.

Mozilla 1.2.x was supposed to have included the new features of Phoenix as well as all the other stuff that Mozilla has. I am a little disappointed that Mozilla has released a new stable and now has an alpha out since Phoenix put out it's version .5. Seems that Phoenix would get a little more attention.

Anyway, the major difference is Phoenix is just a browser. Mozilla is a browser, IRC client, mail reader, newsgroup reader, html editor, download manager, and who knows what else. Phoenix also runs without an install. Just unzip/untar and click the executable.
