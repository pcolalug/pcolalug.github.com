Title: Re: latest projects
Post by: crowe on August 08, 2006, 02:39:00 am
Quote from: bhoff on August 07, 2006, 03:40:22 pm
As to putting load on the 5 and 12 volt rails know It is an old at with on/of button.

Well, that's not quite what I was asking, actually -- I'm curious about this because I've done something similiar, except all I was interested in was having the +12v line available.  I found that putting a load in the form of a couple of parallel resistors across the 5v lines was necessary in order to get the supply's regulator circuit to function properly.

Quote from: bhoff on August 07, 2006, 03:40:22 pm
I am looking to making a variable 0-12vdc output also.

I was lucky enough to find an internal potentiometer in the regulator circuit that would allow me to adjust the supply voltage.  I was able to wind the +12v line up to almost 13.6v this way, which was rather ideal for my purposes.  Of course, the 5v line also went up when I did this, which made the resistors wired across it run that much hotter, but that was something I could live with.
