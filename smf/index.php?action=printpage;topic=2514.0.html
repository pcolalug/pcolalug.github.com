Title: USB key for Mandrake Move
Post by: phed on July 06, 2005, 02:04:14 am
Just a quick observation about using a USB key with Mandrake Move.  Although the Lexar Jump Drive Secure is not on Mandrake's list of compatible USB keys I got it to work fine on both my desktop and laptop by connecting it to a powered USB hub.  The powered hub seems to be the critical factor as neither machine detected the USB key when connected directly to the computer's USB port.

Nice to have something that works you can get at Wal-Mart.  :D
