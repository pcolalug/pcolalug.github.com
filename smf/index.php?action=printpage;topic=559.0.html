Title: Arachnophilia
Post by: dpeach on March 17, 2003, 08:34:09 pm
My favorite HTML editor has been ported to Java! Not that I am a big fan of Java (seen waaay too much of it done poorly), but I love the editor.

You can get Arachnophilia at their website (http://www.arachnoid.com/index.html). I have it running right now. They have made some changes since it was a Windows only program. I have not played with it enough to know if they have just tried to get the basic functionality back that they had before, or if they have actually improved the program.

It is care ware (http://www.arachnoid.com/careware/index.html).

I have liked some of the other editors that I have seen for Linux, but to me there is a warm fuzzy inside of me to see this now available. It is what I am used to using.
