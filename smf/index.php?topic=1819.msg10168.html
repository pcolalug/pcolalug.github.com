I have had it for a few days. I went with VoicePulse. The unlimited local calling was just too good to pass up. I have already talked 187 minutes in 4 days with it! And, I have only talked with 3 people at different times. So I might be a little limited with 500 minutes.

One of the nice features is that you can turn down the quality settings to use less bandwidth. I am fighting a bandwidth war here and I think I should win today or Monday. I am paying for 192 down and 96 up, but am only getting 19 up. Of course I did not know this until I tried to use my phone and things have not been working as planned. So, I can turn the quality setting down from the normal 64k (each direction) to 32 or 16. I have it set at 16 right now and it is just fine. I can tell a quality difference, but it is not terrible.

If I get this worked out to my satisfaction, I should not have any trouble using the web and talking on the phone at the best quality. Right now I am on the lowest quality and cannot even think about touching the web while on the phone. They feel they are obligated to try and get me as close to the 192 as possible (I really only get 154), but have no obligation on the up speed. I have informed them that I disagree. I have talked with three techs before I finally got one to see that that is robbery (and I called it such). He is to have a supervisor call me today.
