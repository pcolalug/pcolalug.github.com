unofficial is one you can typically print off of your college's website.  It has your class information, but it is "unverified" and presented by you and not by your college.

An OFFICIAL transcript is different.  You request that your college send us a copy and they sent it, not you.  That is only required if you are hired for the position, however. The unofficial version that you can print or quickly get a copy from your registrar's office is fine for the application.
