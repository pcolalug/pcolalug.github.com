Title: Great way to get alot of reading material.

Bored and looking to scratch the bookworm itch?

Go to your local college library, and request a book exchange form. (I'm not sure what exactly they're called)

When I was going to a local community college for my pre-eng, I had to do a paper on a technical subject.  Of course I chose computer security.

Anyway, alabama like most states has a statewide .edu library system.  I requested 7 books for a 6 page paper, lol.  I bumped into the local sys admin on the way out, you should have seen his face when he noticed the titles.  He was always a bit wary of me from then on, I have no clue why.  :twisted:
