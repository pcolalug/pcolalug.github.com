Danc I read your post about firebird and evolution. I had the same problem with galeon and I found the solution somewhere on the net. Here is what I had to do to fix it. I think it will work for you too.

Go to gnome control panel and select Advanced > File Types and Programs
Go to internet Services and select Add Services
In Discription put [E-Mail] without brackets
In Protocal put [mailto] must be in lower case
In Programs put [/usr/bin/evolution "%s"]
click Ok or Close

Go to your home directory and edit .gnome/gnome to add the following lines or change as necessary under [URL Handlers]
default-show=firebird "%s"
mailto-show=evoluton "%s"
save and close

That fixed it for me. Hope this helps. BTW KDE is the desktop I use galeon and evoluton with.
