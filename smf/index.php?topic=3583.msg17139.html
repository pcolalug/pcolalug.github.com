Here is the email I got from them.
Quote
Greetings,
My name is Jason King and I am one of the co-chairs of NERD INVASION, which is a Nerd music and Gaming Event being held November 6-7 at the Silver Screen in Pensacola.

We have a lot of interest in holding a Linux install fest the morning of Saturday November 7th. Would this be something you guys are interested in helping out with? I think our attendance is going to be somewhere in the neighborhood of 500.

We've got a lot of people coming in from around the country. And I thought it would be great to get the local lug involved.

If you think you may be interested in helping out, let me know.
