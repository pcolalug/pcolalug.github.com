Title: Book Review for The Web Programming Cd Bookshelf
Post by: trigger on July 21, 2003, 05:53:59 am
One of the biggest deterrents in computers is questions. It is funny how one question can lead to a dozen more. I call this the snowball effect. I discovered that I spend hours daily researching for answers. About a month ago, I came across O’reilly’s Bookshelves. I thought to myself. Here we go all the answers that I want all wrapped up and discounted. Well little did I know what came in the bookshelves. I started reading and noticed a few examples that I could use. So I popped in the CD so I could to try these examples out and up pop the menu. There it was right below the list of books. In blue bold letters “Search All Books.” I thought to myself no way. That would be too easy. Sure enough I could not only search individual books but all of them at once. You don’t even realize the power and speed that gives you. With the use of the CD Bookshelves, my problem solving time has decreased approximately 75%. I recommend any and all of the O’reilly CD Bookshelves.
