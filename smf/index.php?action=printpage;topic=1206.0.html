Title: Sun Solaris 9
Post by: imnes on December 12, 2003, 10:31:03 am
My only problem with solaris x86 is that driver support is bad.  Most hardware drivers are available, just not bundled with it, and usually not free.  As an example, most wireless cards are supported, but the drivers are $100 from a company out of Ft. Walton.  Same for a lot of video drivers, but most people can just use the vesa driver, as long as you don't need accelerated 3D video.
