Title: Re: Apple A Threat To Windows Vista. Re Write Confirmed
Post by: kilar on March 28, 2006, 05:48:59 am
Apple may threaten Microsoft, since more consumers resort to pre-built computers nowadays-- Dell, Sony, Gateway-- instead of buying parts at the store. Apple is probably banking on consumers thinking, "Should I pay $1100 for a Dell desktop, or $1100 for an Apple desktop?" This is assuming prices will equalize between Apples and PCs. If the price of certain Apples does not come down, then people will still stick with PCs. Otherwise, Apple can threaten Microsoft. If there are a lot of consumers who still put together their own systems, or who refuse to buy desktops with OSes preloaded, then Linux may be the one to threaten Microsoft. In other words, Apple may be a contender.
