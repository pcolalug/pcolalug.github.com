Title: New Forum Software
Post by: danc on October 07, 2005, 11:24:58 pm
Hi all,

The LUG forums are about to be shifted over to new software.  We have been using the "phpBB" forum software, and will be changing over to "SMF".  It is similar in looks, and has quite a few additional features that should be fun to use.  One of the biggest reasons for the change is the fact that "phpBB" is often exploited with security flaws, and is kind of a pain to keep protected.

Anyway, the change should be happening in the next day or two, when our SysAdmin and Webmaster get a chance to finish backing up and converting the database over.  Thanks go out to Lloydr and Rugmonster for their hard work on this project.  You will still use your same username and password to log in.  It would probably be a good idea to go through the options/settings/profile to make sure things are the way you want them, and choose some things like your desired theme.

The new software has been tested fairly thoroughly by the Steering Committee and seems to be working nicely.  If you notice any strangeness or have any problems, please make a post about it so we can get it corrected.  Enjoy the forums, and let's get busy posting!
