Our next meeting will be held on the third Thursday of January, the 21st, 2010.  I know this is over a month away right now but I'd like to remind everyone so they can keep their schedule's clear for that date.  As you know meetings are now quarterly instead of monthly.  With fewer of them the importance of these meetings increases, so try to think of attendance as mandatory.


Since this will be the first meeting of a new decade as well, I'd like to see it more of as an event than a typical meeting.  The last time we tried a LAN party was an utter disappointment (the details of which shall not be discussed).  However at Nerd Invasion, me, Ian, and new member Fretsejaz were able to set up a 3-man wireless gaming session of Assault Cube, so I think we can make it happen again in January.  I'll also be preparing a presentation of some sort for the meeting.  If anyone else would also like to do a presentation (possibly on a new toy they end up getting for Christmas) that'd be great as well.


Also, since Thursday is a weeknight this may pose some problems for certain members.  If the LUG would rather see a longer meeting on a Saturday, please discuss it below.



Also:  If anyone ever wishes to talk with someone from the steering committee, both me and gree_dragon37 are in the IRC channel almost every night.
