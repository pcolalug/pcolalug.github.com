Title: VOIP sip to sip
Post by: bhoff on March 19, 2007, 07:43:12 pm
Ok we have a system in house the Xcelerator by virtical and you can make a call to anywhere in the world SIP to SIP without a carrier.
As long as you both have an IP you are good to go. If you want the abiltiy to call land lines then you either need carrier like skype or connect your system to your copper lines. Of course at that time it does not become free. But for a neat alterantive you could send a phone to a person you talk to a lot or a friend around the world or several friends and have group conversations. Very cool! Or you could simply download free soft phones to your computer and get a good headset with mic :)
