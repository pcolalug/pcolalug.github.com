It's that time again! Time for our ever popular LAN Party/Meeting/InstallFest.   

For the meeting portion, we will follow our standard format with a talk by Brett Hoff (bhoff) about setting up Kismet with mySQL/gpsd/gpsdrive/ googleearth/flite speech synthesis Sam Feltus on SonomaSunshine -- his eclectic Python powered website for generating Flash files.

Details:

Where: System Specialists, Inc., at 114 East Wright Street.
When:  November 17th, 4:00pm-10:00pm; 6pm for the Meeting portion.

We hope to see you there!
