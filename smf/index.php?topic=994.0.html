September is the time for electing a new Steering Committee for the coming year. This year, we'll be making nominations via the forums and taking the vote at the September meeting. Please post your nominations in this thread. Be sure to include name and/or forum name and the position you wish to nominate this person for. If you have multiple nominations, please include them in a single post.

You must be present at the September meeting to vote, but you don't have to be there to win.

The Steering Committee is a core group of members who are responsible for the day-to-day running of P'cola LUG. The positions are:

1. President - responsible for inter-organizational contacts, organizing events, and presides over meetings.

2. Vice President - primary assistant to the President and responsible for President's duties, should the President be unavailable.

3. Secretary - responsible for intra-organizational communication, posting meeting notes and announcements. Also responsible for forwarding/replying to email sent to info@pcolalug.org

4. Librarian - responsible for the P'cola LUG library administration, review books ordering and distribution, and primary contact for O'Reilly.

Two other positions, Web-master and Web-admin are also part of the committee and are appointed by same. These folks are responsible for the maintenance of our website and hardware assets respectively. Currently, newton is our web-master and steve is our admin. Also, should no-one be nominated/elected to the position of Librarian, this position may also be appointed.

Please consider your nominations carefully, as these folks will be a large part of the "public" face of P'cola LUG.

Nominations will be accepted until 5:00pm on Friday, September 19th.
