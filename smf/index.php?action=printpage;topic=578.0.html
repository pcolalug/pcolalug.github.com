Title: Office Depot to stop carrying non-XP stuff
Post by: danc on March 31, 2003, 09:49:56 pm
Hmmmm....  I'd like to make a point, and would first throw out a disclaimer that I'm not defending Microsoft at all, and am not a huge fan of Microsoft although I do use some of their products, sometimes.

OK.  While this deal does seem a little shady on the surface, think about this:  There are many other places to buy computer products other than Office Depot.  You can go someplace else.  The comeback to that is probably that this is just the first retailer, and there will be others.  Maybe that's true.  However, exclusive agreements between manufacturers and retailers are not at all uncommon in the business world.  A few examples:  If you want to buy gasoline at a Tom Thumb store, you buy Citgo.  No other choice.  But, you can certainly go just down the road to buy another brand.  Does every clothing store sell Levi's brand jeans?  Nope.  There are many other examples if you think about it.

Anyway, while I would prefer to not see this kind of deal made, I don't think it's illegal or immoral.  This particular store thinks it's best for their future sales, and time will tell if they chose correctly.  With the growing power of Linux perhaps we'll see that they made a bad choice.  

That's my opinion, anyway.  /asbestos-suit mode "on"/
Dan
