To get that printer to work you will need the correct driver which you can probably get from Debian.  There is a cups and lpr version you can download
from http://www.avasys.jp/english/linux_e/dl_ink.html just in case debian doesn't have it.
The driver is pipslite-cups-1.0.0-1.i386.rpm for the cups version and pipslite-1.0.0-1.i386.rpm for the lpr version.  If you not using cups you might have a
problem getting that printer to function.
So good luck and may the swartz be with you.
