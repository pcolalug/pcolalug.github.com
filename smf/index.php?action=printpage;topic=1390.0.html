Title: Cut -n- Paste tip
Post by: dpeach on February 12, 2004, 11:13:59 pm
If you have a middle mouse button, or use 3-button emulation, you can use that middle button for cut and paste. This works in all your apps. Even the command line. This also works in places where  Cntl-C and Cntl-V don't work as you expect them too.

Give it a try and you will find yourself cursing Windows (when you have to use it) because it doesnt do this.
