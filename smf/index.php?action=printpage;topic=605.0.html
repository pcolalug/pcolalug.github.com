Title: Restarting Linux Windows
Post by: steve on April 08, 2003, 09:16:05 pm
OK, here's a neat trick for people that are new to Linux. If you happen to be trucking along in KDE, Gnome, or your favorite Linux Windows manager and things seem to lock up,  you can always try a Ctrl-Alt-BkSpc. This will kill and restart X Windows in Linux. It should also kill all programs you started while in KDE, Gnome, or whatever.

You shouldn't have to use this very often but it's good to know.

BTW, it also reloads things like your video drivers.
