Title: Free DD-WRT Wireless Router
Post by: rugmonster on February 17, 2007, 12:56:46 pm
http://www.fon.com/1yearold/

Share a wireless connection with the public and get a free router. These cool little routers are designed by Cisco/Linksys, and come already flashed and running ddwrt! Plus they allow a dual SSID (public and private), so all you wifi hacks should like that. If you have a business, it's a good way to get a little publicity. There are several places are NWFL already with these hanging out.

Title: Re: Free DD-WRT Wireless Router

I'm fairly certain it's going to be against the TOS of most any commercial ISP.

I personally don't see it as any different than putting up a wireless ap that isn't controlled. Everyone can still connect to it and go. With this, they can connect, but you limit how much bandwidth they can use and they are separated from your network. It's sort of like the whole "no servers" clause in most every cable companies TOS. Yeah, it's there and no they would rather you not do it, but rarely is it enforced. The likelihood of them figuring it out is so low, I'm not even going to worry about it with my ISP. I hate them as it is, so it won't make me cry if they drop my service.
