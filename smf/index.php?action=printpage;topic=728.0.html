If you guys would like this server to be visible to the public, try this:  
1.  You'll need to open ports 27900 and 27950 (both udp) on the firewall.
2.  Add the following to your "public.cfg" (the first line may already be there):
Code:
seta sv_master1 "wolfmaster.idsoftware.com"
seta sv_master2 "master.gamespy.com:27900"
seta sv_master3 "master0.gamespy.com"

These are the master server "browsers" that rebroadcast the available servers to the world.

Post by: rugmonster on June 25, 2003, 12:02:24 pm
Quote from: dpeach
Now my question is, how do people get those insane XPs?


You get XP for a lot of things in ET. Engineers, Medics and Field Ops get lots of XP. As a Medic or Field Ops, they get XP for dropping items, reviving people, throwing air strikes, etc. Engineers get a lot of XP from building stuff and repairing stuff. They are pivotal in most maps and with one or two on a team, they are what get the objectives completed. Combine that with the fact that you get XP for being in the game without dying over time and XP for hits and kills and you can easily rack up some major XP. The key is not getting killed because you actually loose XP when you die.
