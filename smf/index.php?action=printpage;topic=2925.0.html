I thought I would pass this on. August 13 through 17, Auburn University Montgomery will be hosting the 2006 Air Force Information Technology Conference. It's free for active duty, retired active duty, local, state, or federal government employee, government contractor, college/university employee or student with valid identification. For everyone else, it's $100.
