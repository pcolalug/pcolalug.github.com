Hi Members,

Most of you know about our Computer Tech 2010 at the Northwest Florida State College, Niceville, Florida on Saturday, January 23, 2010.  This is our 4th year for this type of computer event for the home computer user.  Many of our SWCTC members attended Expo ’09 and everyone plans to attend on Saturday.  This event is open to the public and is free.

When you arrive at the Northwest Florida State College follow the directional road signs entering the campus using the West Gate.  Follow the signs to the Building K parking area.  Look for the banner over the entrance to Building K. Come through the entrance doors to the College Mall and go to our registration desk on the right side.

Classes start at 8:30 AM so plan to arrive early and register or check in to get a goody bag (one per family) and one door prize ticket per person.  Arriving early will give you enough time to become familiar with classroom locations.  Some classes are sure to fill up before others so if you want to be sure of getting a sit go to the workshop of your choice without delay.  The fire code dictates the seating capacity for each room that we must follow.

The Boys and Girls Club of America and the Secondsplease Cheese Cake Company will offer lunch and cheese cake from 11:30 AM to 1.00 PM at the College Mall (Bldg K).  We have more than 14 Vendors we invite you to visit throughout the day.  This is another great place to get answers to your computer questions and learn new technology.  Look for some of the Tech ’10 specials that are being offered by some vendors.  Some vendors are offering specials on laptop repairs if you bring your laptop to this event and leave it with them.

We have some of the most knowledgeable presenters at Computer Tech 2010.  On your Schedule of Events you have 24 different workshops to choose from. You are encouraged to take notes.  Most all of the presentations will be posted on the http://www.NWFACUG.org web site for your use.

Carl Lofstrom

PS: I expect to see all of our SWCTC computer members on Saturday at Computer Tech 2010.

Local Press Release:

Free Computer Expo for Home Computer Users January 23 at NWF State College

The fourth annual “Computer Tech 2010” will be held Saturday, January 23 from 8:30 am to 4:00 pm at the Northwest Florida State College Niceville campus in the College Mall, Building K.  The free event focuses on the home computer user and will feature 24 free “How-To” workshops, vendor displays and door prizes.  The annual event is the only one of this type in the Florida Panhandle.

Workshop topics include: buying and selling on e-Bay, creative use of photos, internet browser choices, Twitter and Facebook tips, 3D movies for your PC & TV, basic web design, how to stop identity theft, Microsoft Windows 7, and more.  Also, locally popular "The Geek" Jeff Werner will participate.  Werner is the computer columnist with the Northwest Florida Daily News.

The event is designed for the home computer user at all skill levels from beginner to advanced.  For a complete schedule see attachment or visit www.nwfacug.org .  The expo is sponsored by the Association of Personal Computer Users Groups, Northwest Florida Association of Computer User Groups, and NWF State College’s PrimeTime Continuing Education program.
