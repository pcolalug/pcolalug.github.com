August meeting summary
« on: August 28, 2005, 09:33:57 am »
We had a good meeting (and LAN party) yesterday at All Pro Sound.  The ET game server worked well, and there was a lot of fragging done.  We had about 6 people playing on the LAN, and a few others joined us from "outside" (including some international players!).  I must say that the younger crowd seemed to be doing the best...  

There were a few Review Books handed out, and several complimentary copies of the "Make" magazine from O'Reilly.  Some good reading material there.  As for LUG business, members were reminded that next month we plan to hold our annual election for the Steering Committee positions.  I will be making a seperate info post regarding that later today.

There was a presentation on "Command Line Basics" given by yours truly, which hopefully was helpful to some.  It seemed that most of the information was pretty well known by the members in attendance, which is a good thing.  I do plan to do another presentation on more advanced CLI topics at a later date.  If anyone would like a copy of the presentation slideshow, there is a link to it on this page:  http://members.cox.net/danc3/plugpix.htm

As usual, the hosting provided by All Pro Sound was outstanding.  It's a great place for doing a LAN Party, and the projector works perfectly for presentations.  Many thanks to Chris and APS for providing us such a great location.

That's about it for this month.  Please look at the other post I will make regarding the election process.  We have noticed a somewhat downward trend on meeting attendance, and would sure like to see that reversed.  Next month is especially important for a large turnout.  If you missed this one, we sure hope to see you next month!

Dan
PLUG Secretary
