Post by: dpeach on October 26, 2004, 05:47:25 pm
I had been a Joi Internet (http://getjoi.com/new/index.php) user for a while. They let you do a normal dial up connection without making you use their software. They also have a program for Windows, if you want, to take care of the dialing for you. It costs $9.95 per month if you do a month to month plan.

I had a real hard time figuring out how to cancel their service when I moved out of thier service area. I finally found it tucked way deep somewhere. I complained to them about that. I never heard from them as to whether they were going to fix it.

They do have some limitations. You cannot be connected for more than 5 hours straight. If you are idle for more than 10 minutes, you get booted. After your 5 hour limit, or being booted for lack of activity, then you have to wait 1 minute before reconnecting.

For their cheap price this was fair to me. I used it rarely when I was travelling away from home. But never had any problems with them (except cancelling the service.)
