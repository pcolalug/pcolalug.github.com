A note of caution about Lycoris: Device support isn't all encompasing (YMMV). Also, at first blush, it doesn't seem like a "power" distro. Its whole purpose in life is to be easy for your grandma to install and use. Just be sure to grab the dev tools and sources iso's as well.

Oh, and don't use the mirror in the US, as its throttled to hell. I had relatively acceptable speeds from the Sweden mirror.
