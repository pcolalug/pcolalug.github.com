I'm glad things are returning to normal for NWFL and LA. Up here in Montgomery, we didn't get much of anything. For some reason, I did lose power...which was odd considering everything else around my apartment complex had power.

As far as damage goes, my Dad suffered some in Milton. Flying debris took off the driver's side mirror on his Expedition. Apparently, the cover for the chimney stack came loose and finally, after three strong storms, the privacy fence around the backyard is going to have to be replaced. It seems to be doing a major gangsta lean with several boards missing now. When I talked to him last night, he still didn't have power, but was managing fine now that he is prepared with a window a/c unit and a generator strong enough to run it and the fridge. In fact, he was cooking chicken and dumplings with his fish cooker last night.

Rburt3 and family should be coming back down to Milton sometime soon. I don't think there was any major damage to their house, just some downed trees and limbs in the yard. I think I'll be seeing them on their way back down tomorrow. Maybe I can coax a post or two out of him.
