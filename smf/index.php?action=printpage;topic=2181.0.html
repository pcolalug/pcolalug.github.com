Title: Apple MiniMac is Out
Post by: dpeach on January 12, 2005, 10:09:08 am
http://www.apple.com/ipodshuffle/

Simple MP3 player. You can get 512 or 1 GB model. It is like a USB drive. Plug it in and it synchs for you. You can have it randomly synch a bunch of songs off of your computer, or give it a particular playlist. It can also play randomly so that you never know which song is coming next. Charges via the USB when you are plugged in for synching.

$99 for the 512.

Does not even weigh an ounce (.78 ). Goes 12 hours on a charge and has an adaptor to add 2 AAA batteries to it.

Pretty cool. Not 40GB, but since I have had only one CD ripped to my Palm as my MP3 player for the last year, I think 512 is sufficiently large enough for me.
