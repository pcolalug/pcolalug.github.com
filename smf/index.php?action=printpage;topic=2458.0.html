Title: Remote Connection Question
Post by: lloydr on June 01, 2005, 11:39:05 am
if your trying to connect to a specific port from the outside nad map that port back to a certain LAN machine, then you use NAT which should be included inside your westell modem/router if you browse to the modem's LAN ip which is generally 192.168.1.1 , 192.168.0.1 , 192.168.1.254 , or 192.168.0.254 whichever is your gateway address that dhcp is assigning.

If your doing this all on the LAN side, from a LAN LTSP machine to a LAN linux machine then you simply put in the LAN dhcp lease address, or you can statically assign the machines on the LAN an address that is on the same LAN as the modem but out of the dhcp lease range.
