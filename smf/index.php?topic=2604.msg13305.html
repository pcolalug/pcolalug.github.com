Well,

I actually did manage to get it "working" thanks to a guy on alt.os.slackware, with the same problem.  Turns out the nvidia framebuffer driver (NOT RIVA) was conflicting with the *76 video driver.  I had everything set, rebooted, and lo and behold I had video.  

However, I could obviously tell something was up with my video.  So I did xorgconfig with my *known good* values for my lcd, I pop back into X, and I get "out of sync" errors.  Well now, I know what your thinking, "those were the wrong sync values", negative.  

Those were the exact specs for that lcd pulled from the website many moons ago, and they worked flawlessly till now.  What we have here, gentilemen, is a borked driver.  I'll simply have to wait till nvidia releases another driver.
