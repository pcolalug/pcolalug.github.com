182  Distributions / RPM Based Distributions / RH9 & CUPS on: May 07, 2003, 11:39:34 am
As the villiage idiot of the LUG I'm pleased to announce a minor miracle.  I've got RH9 running on the mighty SANDSPUR two-PC LAN and have a printer on box #1.  The two machines are connected through a switch and the network was working with SSH OK.

The printer on box #1 was all working --- configured with a new CUPS GUI.  I had been trying to get the printer to be shared.

Was working on the configuration for the printer on box #2 and applied the changes and sent out a test page.  Nothing happened.  Went to a terminal and typed 'lpq' and it said the job was in progress.  Still nothing.  Was starting to cancel the job manually and thinking about getting down the .20 guage loaded with bunny rabbit shot when I was scared out of my skin when the printer started making noise.

Most amazing!  Grog for all hands.
