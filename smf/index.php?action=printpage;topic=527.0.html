Title: Which distro?
Post by: rugmonster on March 09, 2003, 08:20:39 pm
I have said before my history with distros. I have used everything from Slackware to Gentoo with Debian, Redhat, Mandrake, SuSE and Caldera in between. I voted for Gentoo because I think they have one of the absolute best package management systems. I think the package management is one of the only things that really sets a distro apart from the others. Yes, the packages are important along with the added tools, but this all comes down to the way the packages are put together. Gentoo takes the best ideas of debian, but brings in the best aspects of ports and keeps up with the bleed edge of the available software. I think it is by far the superior way to handle package management.
