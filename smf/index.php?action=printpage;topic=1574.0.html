Title: Firewall Script Generators
Post by: rburt3 on April 26, 2004, 10:19:19 pm
Google for fwBuilder and/or FirewallBuilder. Its a popular GUI tool for, as the creative name sugguests, building firewall rules. What's kinda cool about it is that once you've layed out your rules, the tool can generate iptables and/or pf (OpenBSD's stateful packet filter/NAT utility) rules.
