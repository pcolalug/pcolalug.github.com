Title: Has anyone setup a serial connection?
Post by: hitechwizard on August 25, 2003, 09:19:24 pm
agetty and a null serial cable is what I used to connect my Wyse terminal to my Redhat box. Worked like a champ. Check w/crowe, as he now has the terminal to see what the pinout should be. Something like 2-3, 3-2, 7-7  and 20-20 (for 25pin) should be all you need (You can surf the net to get the full pinout).

Title: Has anyone setup a serial connection?
Post by: crowe on August 26, 2003, 03:21:13 am
I currently have a wyse terminal hooked up to one of my machines...  Like it's already been said, you'll need a null modem cable -- I don't recall just how I made mine offhand; your best bet for that would be to either google (http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8&q=null-modem+cable&btnG=Google+Search) for a wiring schematic, or to go purchase a prefabricated one.

You might also need to install agetty or the like to make it work -- If I recall correctly, Redhat only comes with mingetty, which won't work over a serial line.


Hope this helps!
