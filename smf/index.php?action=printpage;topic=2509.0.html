Title: Any difference between Mandrake 10.1 and Mandriva?
Post by: danc on July 04, 2005, 07:34:00 am
The newest version is Mandriva LE 2005, which is what would have been Mandrake 10.2.  They changed the name at the same time as they were due to release 10.2 and decided to change the version numbering also.  From now on they will be releasing only once a year.  Mandriva 2006 is due out in the fall.

So, to fully answer your question, what you bought is a version behind...  To tell the truth, there isn't that much difference anyway.  If I were you I'd get that installed, and wait for the 2006 version for your next upgrade.
