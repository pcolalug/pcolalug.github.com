Title: Free Asterisk book from O'Reilly
Post by: dpeach on February 28, 2007, 07:51:52 am
We have had some discussion about interest in Asterisk (http://www.pcolalug.org/smf/index.php?topic=3005.0). About 2 years ago bhoff asked me if I would be interested in getting an Asterisk setup going to make calls back to the US easier. At the time I had just signed up with a VoIP provider and was (and still am) very pleased with the service and saw no need for it.

While doing research for a side project, I found this gem online (http://www.asteriskdocs.org/modules/tinycontent/index.php?id=11). It is a PDF of a book that O'Reilly (http://promosearch.atomz.com/search/promosearch?sp-q=asterisk&sp-a=sp1000a5a9&sp-f=ISO-8859-1&sp-t=general&sp-x-1=cat&sp-q-1=&sp-x-2=cat2&sp-q-2=&sp-c=25&sp-p=all&sp-k=Articles%7CBooks%7CConferences%7COther%7CWeblogs&c=&p=&query=asterisk&submit.x=0&submit.y=0) released about Asterisk (http://www.asterisk.org). It was released under the Creative Commons license (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/) (by-nc-nd). I love O'Reilly more every day. It is all about Asterisk and getting it going for you.

I still am not sure that Asterisk will fit my needs, but I am looking into it and thought others would enjoy knowing about this book.
