Like I have said so many times before, I hang out in #linux on EFNet. To get into #linux you need to have identd enabled. The way you can check for that is when you get connected to a server, type /who <nick> and look for a tilde (~) in front of your information. Also, to get on efnet, you can either type into your irc client /server irc.efnet.net as this is a round robin dns name or go look on the efnet site for a server. *Hint* Stay away from irc.prison.net...

Oh yeah, there is also Freenode. This is a whole network mainly for open projects. Many of our favorite distributions have official and unofficial channels there.

If you ever want to look me up, I go by Ruggy and mainly stick to efnet and freenode.
