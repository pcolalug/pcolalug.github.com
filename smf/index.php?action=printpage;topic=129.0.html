Title: 3.0.3 is cold. How 'bout 3.1!
Post by: dpeach on August 22, 2002, 08:54:04 pm
3.1beta was released today. Read the press release (http://www.kde.org/announcements/announce-3.1beta1.html).

I really like some of the things I am seeing with this release. Here are some screenshots (http://static.kdenews.org/mirrors/qwertz/kde31alpha/) from the alpha release. Konquerer is looking great. I have never really liked it as a browser, but it is starting to look more mature. I do not know how well it conforms to the standards. That will take some investigation. As a Mozilla user, I love "tabbed browsing." Konquerer will now have that feature. Kmail is supposed to have some more robustness added.

On the devlopers site are the  feature plans  (http://developer.kde.org/development-versions/kde-3.1-features.html)for 3.1. You can see what has already been completed and what still needs work before the final release. Mid-October is the scheduled final release.
