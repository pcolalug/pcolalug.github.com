Title: Nokia N800
Post by: L.Rentz on January 01, 2008, 10:32:04 am
Happy New Year everyone.  I decided to purchase the Nokia N800 as a Christmas present to myself.  I have been tinkering with it for a few days now.  I have to say I am impressed with what you can do with it.  Its very flexible.

The first think I did was install OS2008 which will hinder it for a little while while packages get ported from the earlier OS releases.  But overall its pretty cool.  It is amazing how good the connectivity is.  The N800 finds and connects to wifi very fast and easy.  When I do not have wifi available, I have the N800 tethered via bluetooth to my cell which has unlimited internet usage (at dialup speeds until 3G is available here), its pretty quick though.

I have a Wintec bluetooth gps logger that connected without a hitch for use with maemo mapper.

The N800 has a little vga webcam that I have not really used yet.  I think at this time the only service that is setup to use it is the gizmo project.  I did set up an account, so if anyone uses the gizmo project and would like to do a test let me know.

I have used google talk and skype messengers with it (less video) and it works really well.

Lee
