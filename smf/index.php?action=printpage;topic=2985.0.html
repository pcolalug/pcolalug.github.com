Title: 2006 - 2007 Steering Committee Nominations and Election
Post by: danc on August 21, 2006, 12:04:35 pm
Hi all,

It's that time of year again.  I have cut-n-pasted much of the same message from last year regarding the nomination and election process.  Please read it and participate.

It's time again to nominate Steering Committee members for the coming year.  Please post your nominations in this thread.  Be sure to include name (and/or forum name) and the position you wish to nominate this person for.  If you have multiple nominations, please include them in a single post.

The Steering Committee is a core group of LUG members who are responsible for the day-to-day running of P'cola LUG. The positions are:

1. President - responsible for inter-organizational contacts, organizing events, and presides over meetings.  Incumbent:  danc

2. Vice President - primary assistant to the President and responsible for President's duties, should the President be unavailable.   Incumbent:  imnes

3. Secretary - responsible for intra-organizational communication, posting meeting notes, summaries, and announcements. Also responsible for forwarding/replying to email sent to info@pcolalug.org.   Incumbent:  green_dragon37

4. Librarian - responsible for the P'cola LUG library administration, "review book" ordering and distribution, and primary contact for O'Reilly.  Incumbent: lloydr

Two other positions - Webmaster and SysAdmin - are also part of the Steering Committee and are appointed by same. These folks are responsible for the maintenance of our website and hardware assets respectively.  These two positions can be held by a single person.  Incumbents:  Webmaster: Rugmonster    SysAdmin: lloydr
 
If you are nominated, please carefully consider your acceptance. These positions take some time, effort, and commitment on your part. It's not much, really, but please consider your other obligations. Talk to some of the current and/or former Committee members if you have specific questions about a position.  Once you have decided to accept (or decline) the nomination, please post a reply in this thread to that effect!

Nominations will be accepted from now until the 15th of September. We will vote as a group at the September meeting (9/16). You must be present at the September meeting to vote, but you don't have to be there to win.  The new committee should have all appointed positions filled and officially assume its duties on October 1st.

Please take a minute to consider who you'd like to see elected to help our LUG function and grow, and PLEASE set aside some time to make it to the September meeting to VOTE!

Thanks,
Dan
President, P'cola LUG
