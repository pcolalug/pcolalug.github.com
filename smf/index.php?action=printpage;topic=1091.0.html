Title: 3. Library
Post by: rburt3 on May 04, 2004, 10:16:32 pm
Members in good standing shall have the opportunity to create an account with the Pensacola Linux User's Group lending library.

Library books are for the use and education of the entire group. If books are neglected or are not returned in a reasonable time frame, the offending member may be barred from the library. A member who fails to return a book for an extended period of time, severely damages materials, or otherwise abuses books belonging to the Pensacola Linux User's Group or members thereof may be required to replace damaged or lost material. In severe cases, the offending user may be banned from membership and participation in the Pensacola Linux User's Group.

As a sponsor, O'Reilly allows members to request free books, provided that the requester write a review of the book and publish same in the Pensacola Linux User's Group forums, as well as two-or-three other relevant sites (Slashdot, Amazon, etc). Should a member fail to complete a review in a timely manner, the book in question may revert to another member, or the general library. This is so that the Pensacola Linux User's Group can maintain it's advantageous relationship with O'Reilly and continue to receive materials beneficial to all members.
