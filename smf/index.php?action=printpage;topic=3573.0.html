Title: September 2009 Meeting
Post by: dpeach on September 17, 2009, 08:35:15 pm
We had a good meeting tonight with 5 in attendance. We talked a bit about the elections coming up next month and a few other club topics.

Mainly we talked about the NX Free software. While a few at the meeting already have Log-Me-In and Go To Meeting accounts, the combination of Hamachi and NX Free accomplish the same task for Linux. (Hamachi is now owned by Log-Me-In). I was very interested in this presentation by Ed since it particularly fulfills a need that I have in my own network.

While we struggled a bit to show the full power of the software over the Internet (because of firewall restrictions), Ed did a good job of getting it set up quickly to show on the local network. Thanks Ed.
