Title: Foil Disk Platters
Post by: rugmonster on October 27, 2006, 10:34:30 am
In case you missed it on Slashdot, Cringley is promoting a new business venture he is embarking on with a couple of very smart friends. (http://www.pbs.org/cringely/pulpit/2006/pulpit_20061026_001143.html)

By using a metal foil for the disk platters rather than aluminum or glass, spin up time is reduced, power consumption is reduced, spindle speeds can be increased, data density can be increased and durability is increased. This is the first new technology in recent times that has really excited me. A very interesting read.
