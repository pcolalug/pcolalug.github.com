Title: IDS up and running!
Post by: bhoff on June 24, 2007, 02:51:12 pm
Well I finally got IDS up and running where I want it after a long time of playing with it.
Running an OpenBSD 4.1 box with SNORT/MySQL/BASE/Oinkmaster.
Was some real fun getting everything up and running right. But we are there.
Rules are updating like they should using Oinkmaster and alerts are feeding into BASE which give me some really useful
mapping of attack vectors and types of attacks along with graph generation that can be inserted easily into a client side webpage.
I am running two nics 1 on the enemy side and one on the inside for management functions and looking at the webpage.

Will update as I add or tweak and will post my notes here once I go over them and clean them up a bit.
