Quote from: Brent on April 30, 2006, 12:59:58 pm
Ubuntu's wifi setup is really fast and easy compared to all of the manual configuration you have to do with other distros.

Not much to it, really.  All I have to do on Slack is untar the madwifi tarball, do a "make" and a "make install", and then paste the following into my /etc/rc.d/rc.local:

Code:
ifconfig ath0 down
iwconfig ath0 essid "xxxxxxxx" channel 5 mode Managed key XXXXXXXXXX
sleep 1
ifconfig ath0 192.168.254.50 netmask 255.255.255.0
sleep 1
ifconfig ath0 up
sleep 1
route add default gw 192.168.254.254 dev ath0
