The Linux InstallFest is coming up here in a little over a week, I got a little information from Chris at All Pro Sound with working out the details of the networking side of things.

We are planning to have a lan party going on to show off the gaming abilities of Linux along with keep ourselves busy if it gets slow or waiting for installs to finish.  

As far as networking, Chris is kind enough to allow us to use spare 24port 10/100 switches (1 probaly being enough). He's going to run a line back for us from their OC3 connection into the conference room, most likely into a normal little router so we can have the gameserver on the lan and on the outside by mapping the ports back without having to use 2 network cards on the box.

Chris gave me an outside ip address to use so I could go ahead and map any dns entries to the ip, for anyone on the outside who wants to join in.

During the Installfest, those of you who aren't able to make it, should be able to reach the gameserver with:
