Tonight was the first time I could actually go to a meeting and meet the people behind the nicknames on the forums.

I was very impressed by the entire experience and would like to submit this thought.

In our group, there are a number of people, some experienced, some gurus, some newbies.

All of us prefer to use Linux over other operating systems.

We all, by default, are Linux advocates.

This, being the case, makes us all responsible to promote Linux to the Florida panhandle, as well as any other area that a member lives in.

The linux.org website has a HOW-TO in their documentation section.
Let's all take a look at it and try to come up with a unified plan to to
promote Linux as an alternative to the other OS's
