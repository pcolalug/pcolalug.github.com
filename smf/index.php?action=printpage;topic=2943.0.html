Title: The Soft Side of Networking
Post by: rugmonster on July 25, 2006, 06:42:47 pm
There have been numerous times that I've had a question or comment about networking that just didn't fit under the Networking Hardware section. I thought it was about time we had a section for networking under Software.

Relevant topics for this section include, but are not limited to:

IP Networking Concepts and Questions (DHCP, IP Subnetting, etc)
Network Infrastructure Services such as DNS, Mail Servers, NIS, NFS, Samba, FTP, SSH, etc
Firewall Technology such as IPTables, pf and the various third party tools
IP Routing and Routing Protocols
VPN Technologies such as IPSec and OpenVPN

Basically, anything that has to do with networking outside of cabling, switches and dedicated networking appliances should go in here.
