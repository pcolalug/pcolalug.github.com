I am fan of Linspire (Lindows).  I've become a lifetime member and run it on several machines.  I like the software projects they're working on (the NVU html editor being one).  They've just released a new media player for linux called LSongs.

Here's some screen caps:
http://images.linspire.com/applications/3373/12323/lsongs/lsongs.jpg
http://images.linspire.com/applications/3373/12323/lsongs/lsongs1.jpg
http://images.linspire.com/applications/3373/12323/lsongs/lsongs3.jpg

It's available for Linspire users for free (there's a charge for non-members to get it).

Lsongs is a music manager and media player for Linspire. This all-in-one program installs easily with CNR Technology and offers great features for any audiophile:

 * Import audio tracks from your CDs
 * Organize your music collection using play lists and more
 * Listen to MP3s, audio CDs, or hundreds of streaming Internet radio stations
 * Burn custom music CDs in one simple step

More details at: http://info.linspire.com/lsongs/
