Title: Better way to search eBay?
Post by: dpeach on October 16, 2005, 09:29:16 pm
Not sure if this site is better (http://the-winning-bid.com/) than the eBay search is, but it is definately a step in the right direction.

Do a search and you get the results with photos. You can specify your price range. Which helps if you want to look for the price of say a car but don't want to get all of the results for the parts of the car. You can say that the item must be at least $100 before it shows.

Simple interface. Less confusing than eBay itself. Has some promise, but unless they really get on the ball and make some changes, then advertise themselves, they probably won't make it. I got this off of www.digg.com which is good advertisement for them, but I think they will need to show some improvement quickly to get anywhere. Currently they are being /.ed. Not sure if digg is causing this or if they have been picked up by /. too.
