Title: Mandriva 2008

I had been using Debain Etch on my laptop for a year. It was a pain to setup but worked more or less. Occasional lock ups, Configure sound, mouse pad didn't work right, Didn't detect the wireless device, installing Flash and a bunch of other stuff. anyway....
I installed Mandriva 2008 a month ago and it has been really nice. 
I did find a few things... Totem and lindvd have issues, so I use Mplayer.
The audio jack for headphones still doesn't work. It looks like it doesn't work for most distros, I think its an Alsa thing. So I had to setup Mplayer to us my USB headphones.
I did discover I have a wireless device... Mandriva detected it but didn't load the drivers which it has on the cd. I bought this super cheap laptop and I didn't check if it had wireless, didn't really need it until I was stuck in a few airports, DOH!  Some day when I can get to a place that has wireless I'll check ad see if it works.
I didn't have to download anything extra. 
I also installed it on my computer at home and everything worked perfect.
