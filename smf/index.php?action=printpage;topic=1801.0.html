I'm not sure if anyone here is into MUDs but I just got back into them when I heard about  Achaea (http://www.achaea.com) on ScreenSavers.

For those that don't know what a MUD is, it stands for "Multi-User Dungeon".
It's basically a text based RPG.  You can find more info at The Mud Faq (http://www.mudconnect.com/mudfaq/).
They actually date back to 1978 as you can see on  the Online World Timeline (http://www.legendmud.org/raph/gaming/mudtimeline.html)

Anyway, I just got into Achaea and it's a pretty cool world.

For those that are currently playing Muds:
Which ones do you frequent?
How long have you been playing?
What's the best Mud client for Linux? (Anything close to Zmud?)

Thanks,
Steve
