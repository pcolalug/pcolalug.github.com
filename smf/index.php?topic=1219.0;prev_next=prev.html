Try to look for menu.lst, this is the old grub.conf.  Also, your framebuffer and splash are part of the initrd specified after the running kernel line in menu.lst. In the line for the booting kernel in menu.lst, you should also see the vesa=... line. You can change that to vga= and the apropriate mode number. Also, you could get rid of this functionality altogether by removing framebuffer console from your kernel.

One question, does SuSE 9 run the 2.6 kernel? I thought the framebuffer was still borked.

Personally, I really like the framebuffer console, as you can get smaller and cleaner fonts, as well as some niifty wallpaper
