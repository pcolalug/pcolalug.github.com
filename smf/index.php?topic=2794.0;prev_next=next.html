Hey!

I was brainstorming, and came up with an idea to add something else to the meetings.  There are hundreds of Linux distributions out there, each with their merits, so I figured that I, personally, could review one a month for the Group.  It would be an additional presentation to the current one, and hopefully wouldn't take more than 30 minutes to do each meeting.  The way I plan to do this is install it the day after the meeting, and use it for a month, noting the pros and cons.  At the next meeting, I'll do a short demo with the actual Distro, showing off what I believe to be its strongest point.  If anyone was impressed enough, I could send them home with the ISO to burn then and there.  I would like to do this each month, as long as I can keep it going.  I already have an idea of which Distro I wish to feature first!

Please reply with your ideas and which Distros you'd like to see featured!
