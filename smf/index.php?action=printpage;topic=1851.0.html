Title: hotplug no more
Post by: Brent on September 01, 2004, 07:22:32 pm
Hi all.

I've noticed lately that slack 10's hotpluging really slows down booting on my computer (by like 20+ seconds).  I'm sure it's a specific probe that's causing the slow down, I have plenty of ram.

Anyway, I didn't quite know how to track down which module was giving me trouble, so I disabled hotplug.  After a quick edit of rc.modules to load my eth0 I'm booting a full 22 seconds faster.  Now that's an improvement!

On a side note, I still need to figure out what to modprobe for usb mass storage.  I'll look into it.  Other than that i don't think there have been any side effects from not using hotplug.
