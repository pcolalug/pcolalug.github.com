Title: Data Center in a Box
Post by: imnes on October 18, 2006, 07:51:05 am
Sun has released Project Black Box.  It's a modular data-center built in a standard shipping container.  Just sit it someplace, add power, water and communications and you're set. 

  • 250 Sun Fire T1000 servers with the CoolThreads technology with 2000 cores and 8000 simultaneous threads of execution (or 250 x64 servers with 1000 cores)
  • Up to 1.5 petabytes of disk storage or 2 petabytes of energy-efficient tape storage
  • Up to 7 terabytes of memory
  • Up to up to 10,000 simultaneous desktop users
  • Sufficient power and cooling to support 200 kilowatts of rackmounted equipment

And thanks to the rebate from Pacific Gas and Electric (http://www.sun.com/emrkt/energy-rebate/), customers could get $175,000.00 - $250,000.00 in savings per Black Box.
