You also might consider Lycoris for the desktop.  I played with the Amythist release quite a bit back in the day, and I have to say that the install is even easier than Mandrake.  It looks nice and has some great management apps.  Fair warning, though: this is not a developer distro out of the box.  You can download a separate iso that has most of the common dev libs and such if you need that sort of thing (and who doesn't).  I usually recommend it over Mandrake to folks just starting out, though that may change once I get around to finishing my eval of OEOne, which is, somewhat ironically, a RedHat-only kind of desktop.
