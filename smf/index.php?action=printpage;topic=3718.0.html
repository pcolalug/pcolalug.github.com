If you haven't heard already, the 2010 Southeast User Group Leader Summit (SEUGLS) is taking place on October 16th, 2010 from 11am to 7pm. Register here: http://seugls.eventbrite.com/

This unconference-style event is for technology-based user and community groups in the Southeast. The goal is to share tips and discuss the issues and challenges facing technology groups regardless of the actual technology the group is focused on, such as finding locations, getting speakers, seeking out sponsorship, growing membership, hosting special events, etc.

Microsoft will be hosting at their regional headquarters in Alpharetta, GA (north Atlanta area). The wiki from last year is still running if you'd like to check it out here http://seugls2009.pbworks.com/

For more information and discussion:

Event Wiki: http://seugls.pbworks.com/ - feel free to add session ideas, etc.
