Title: Need Help with Baldur's Gate II
Post by: rugmonster on September 09, 2004, 07:14:48 pm
Even better HOWTO

http://www.linuxdocs.org/HOWTOs/CDServer-HOWTO/procedure.html

Once you have the iso on your local disk, you can then mount it on the loop device (hope you included that in your kernel)

Code:
mount -o loop -t iso9660 -r </path/to/file.iso> </path/to/mount/point>


This will mount the file.iso to your specified mount point read-only. Hope this helps
