Title: Doom 3
Post by: danc on January 12, 2005, 06:44:49 pm
Well, I finally finished Doom3 tonight...  I hadn't been playing it in quite a while, and resumed it last week.  All I can say is:  WOW.  

My thoughts on the game:
The first 20% was very good, fresh, new, scary.
The next 30% got quite repetitive and predictable, and caused me to stop playing for several months, as I just kinda lost interest.
The last 50% (and especially the final 20%) was absolutely fantastic.  I jumped out of my seat many times, and there were some puzzles to be solved...  The "Hell" level was truly amazing.  There were some cutaway "movie clips" at key times, which are just really great.  At the final boss part I wasn't sure I could even do it, but it actually only took me 5 tries...  hehe

To summarize, the game starts well, gets somewhat boring in the middle, and then finishes in a MOST EXCELLENT fashion.  I'd give it a 9 out of 10 on the overall scale, and highly recommend it.  Now, on to Half Life 2...

Dan
