What is the P'cola LUG?

We are a local, non-profit group of Linux users helping each other with Linux (and other computer related) issues. We also actively spread the word about free and open source software.

When and where do you meet?

The Pensacola Linux User's Group meets at 6:00 pm every third Saturday of the month at Systems Specialists on 114 E. Wright St.. The date of our next meeting is posted on the main page.

What does it cost?

Membership in the P'cola LUG is free. There are no club dues.

How do I sign up?

Membership is based on joining the forums.
